Minimum time separation for departing aircraft which are using: an intermediate part of the same runway; or. an intermediate part of a parallel runway separated by less than 760 m (2 500 ft).. is 3 minutes between a HEAVY aircraft when taking off behind a SUPER aircraft. a LIGHT or MEDIUM aircraft when taking off behind a HEAVY aircraft. a LIGHT aircraft when taking off behind a MEDIUM aircraft. and 4 minutes between a LIGHT or MEDIUM aircraft when taking off behind a SUPER aircraft.
