The Organisational Influences level is divided into three categories. Resource Management: Refers to the organisational-level decision-making regarding the allocation and maintenance of organisational assets (e.g., human resources, monetary/budget resources, equipment/facility recourse).. Organisational Climate: Refers to the working atmosphere within the organisation (e.g., structure, policies, culture).. Operational Process: Refers to organisational decisions and rules that govern the everyday activities within an organisation (e.g., operations, procedures, oversight)..
