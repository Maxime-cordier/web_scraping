The Electronic Horizontal Situation Indicator (EHSI), often referred to as the Navigation Display (ND), replaces a number of different instruments found on a conventional aircraft instrument panel, and may be used to depict some or all of the following information: heading flown;. heading or track selected;. bearing to or from a navigation beacon (VHF Omnidirectional Radio Range (VOR), Distance Measuring Equipment (DME));. lateral deviation from a selected track;. ground speed, distance and time to go;. aeronautical map;. weather information;. plus much more information according to design.
