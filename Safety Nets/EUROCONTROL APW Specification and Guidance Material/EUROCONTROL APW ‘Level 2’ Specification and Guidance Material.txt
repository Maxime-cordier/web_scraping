The EUROCONTROL APW Specification describes the minimum requirements for the development, configuration and use of APW in Europe. The Specification exists only as the final draft version while the Guidance Material has a ‘released’ status. Currently, there is no plan to undertake any further development of the Specification. EUROCONTROL Specification for Area Proximity Warning Draft Comprehensive guidance material to assist in implementing the EUROCONTROL Specification for Area Proximity Warning covers the full APW lifecycle: The APW Guidance is provide with several appendices to support ANSPs in the implementation of APW. EUROCONTROL Guidance Material for Area Proximity Warning Released Appendix A: Reference APW System: Detailed technical explanation of typical implementation details of APW with emphasis on parameterisation and performance optimisation. Optimisation concepts are also covered in detail. Appendix B: Safety Assurance: A set of three documents that can be used as starting point for APW safety assurance work in a particular local context. Appendix C: Cost Framework: Appendix D-1: Case Study: Enhancement of APW for ATCC Semmerzake: Identifies potential solutions for a number of issues.
