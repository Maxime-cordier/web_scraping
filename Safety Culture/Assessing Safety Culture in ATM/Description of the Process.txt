The Safety Culture assessment process entails the use of both qualitative and quantitative techniques to capture the nature and essence of the operational context under evaluation. The process involves: a pre-launch phase to prepare the ANSP senior managers and personnel for the assessment and to obtain their support. data collection phases, involving both quantitative processes (questionnaire survey) and qualitative processes (interviews, workshops / focus groups, site visits, reviews of historical information and contextual data analysis, etc.), and, depending on time and accessibility,. Safety Culture analysis. Diagnosis, feedback and the way ahead..
