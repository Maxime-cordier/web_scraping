Flight crew should always record occurrences as defects in the Aircraft Technical Log after flight. This will provide usable data and ensure that a technical asessment/response is made, recorded and signed for.. Pilots and ATCOs report any equipment malfunction, including suspected sleeping receiver, using the national occurrence reporting system, to enable trends to be detected.. Operators and ATCUs report equipment malfunctions to the manufacturer to allow improvements to be made to equipment.. National authorities continue to investigate sleeping receivers in order to develop solutions..
