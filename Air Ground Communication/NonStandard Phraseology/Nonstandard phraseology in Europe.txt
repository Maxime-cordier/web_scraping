Regulation 2016/1185 introduces some deviations from the standard ICAO phraseology at EU level: The UK CAA has adopted certain non-standard phraseology designed to reduce the chance of mishearing or misunderstanding RTF communications. This phraseology is not in accordance with ICAO but is based on careful study of the breakdown of pilot/controller communications.
