An aircraft receives and acts on a clearance intended for another aircraft, in consequence of which: The aircraft takes up a heading or routing intended for the other; or,. The aircraft commences a climb or descent to a level to which it has not been cleared; or,. The aircraft departs the RTF frequency; or,. In responding to the message, the aircraft blocks a transmission from the intended recipient; or,. The intended recipient does not receive the clearance, and fails to take up the desired heading or routing, or fails to climb or descent to the desired level; or,. The workload of ATCOs and pilots is increased due to the necessity to resolve the confusion..
