This initiative explains how single-pilot operations can mitigate risk of many types of GA accidents by closely adhering to official guidance on personal criteria for stabilised approaches, including safe go-around maneuvers. Accidents involving single-pilot flights can be prevented by emphasising the effects of wind on traffic pattern operations during flight reviews and transition training. Particular emphasis should be placed on the turn from base to final..
