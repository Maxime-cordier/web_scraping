The effects of loss of control may include: Discomfort or injury to the occupants prior to recovery to controlled flight.. Structural damage to, or total loss of, the aircraft.. Fatal or serious injury to occupants due to terrain impact and/or post impact fire.. The effects of loss of control depend on the ability of the pilots to recover from the situation. This, in turn, depends on: The nature of the upset causing loss of control;. The experience and ability of the pilots; and,. The height of the aircraft being adequate..
