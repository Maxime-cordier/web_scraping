In all cases, the hijacking dramatically increases the stress level for the entire crew. Amongst other implications are: Possible injuries to, or death of, one or more crew members;. Injuries to, or death of, passengers or hijackers;. Incompatibility between hijacker demands and flight safety;. Complications due to diversion from planned route or altitude - fuel deficiency, Airspace Infringement, landing at unsuitable airport;. Prolonged hostage situation with additional complications such as lack of water, food and medical supplies..
