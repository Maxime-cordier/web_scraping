The omission of a required action or an inappropriate action is the most frequent causal factor in incidents and accidents as illustrated by Table 1. These omissions or errors are often the result of an interruption or distraction. Also, the US Aviation Safety Action Program (ASAP) reveals that 14 percent of crew reports include references to an interruption or distraction (Source: US ASAP - 2000-2001).
