Factors which contribute to the risk of a level bust occurring: Only one pilot/crew member on the ATC frequency;. ATCO work-load;. Interruption/distraction;. Holding Patterns;. Airspace Procedure/Design;. Pilot Workload;. Low atmospheric pressure setting;. Expectation of a particular level;. Deviation from SOPs.. Factors which increase the risk of collision following a level bust: Volume of traffic.. High Rates of Climb or Descent;.
