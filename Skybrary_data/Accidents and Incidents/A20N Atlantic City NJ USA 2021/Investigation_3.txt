The redesigned fan blade is expected to be available in 2023 and further action to directly address the fuel-fed fire risk by better protecting the integrity of the fuel tube is also being developed although final design details and the timing of for this was not known before the investigation of this event was completed. The Final Report was published on 5 July 2022. No Safety Recommendations were made.
