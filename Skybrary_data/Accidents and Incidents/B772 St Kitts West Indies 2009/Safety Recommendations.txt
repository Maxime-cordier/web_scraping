Three Safety Recommendations were made as a result of the Investigation as follows: That the Eastern Caribbean Civil Aviation Authority ensure that Robert L Bradshaw International Airport, St Kitts, establishes a Safety Management System for its airfield operations.. (2010-047) That the Eastern Caribbean Civil Aviation Authority ensures that the infrastructure of Robert L Bradshaw International Airport, St Kitts, complies with ICAO Annex 14 Standards and Recommended Practices or any differences are filed. In the interim a NOTAM of outstanding deficiencies should be published.. (2010-048) That British Airways review the process by which all new destination airfields are inspected to identify any operational issues.. (2010-049) The Final Report of the Investigation was published on 2 September 2010.
