On 27 September 2007, a Beech 90 King Air being operated single pilot by a small UK air taxi operator on a day cargo flight from Southend UK to Zurich left the runway after unintentionally touching down at destination without the landing gear extended following an approach in day Visual Meteorological Conditions (VMC). The pilot, the only occupant, was uninjured but the aircraft was declared a hull loss because of substantial damage.
