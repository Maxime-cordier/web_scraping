A Risk Factor was identified as “the small parts of the aircraft found on the runway during a runway inspection performed hours later” although it was noted that this debris “did not contribute to the origin and history of the accident”. One Safety Recommendation was made as follows: that the European Aviation Safety Agency (EASA) together with the aircraft manufacturer should ensure that it is no longer possible to attach the valve input lever of the hydraulic Differential Control Selector Valve (DCSV) incorrectly.
