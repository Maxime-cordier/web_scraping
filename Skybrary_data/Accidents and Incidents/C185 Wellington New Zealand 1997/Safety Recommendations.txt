Two Safety Recommendations were made to the National Aviation Authority (NAA) that they should: Ensure that waivers of the wake turbulence separation standards are restricted to Visual Meteorological Conditions (VMC) / day only, are required to be acknowledged by pilots using promulgated phraseology and are not permitted to be granted at all under certain specified circumstances.. Publish another article on wing-tip vortices in their safety magazine so as to alert pilots and operators to wake turbulence concerns by highlighting the dangers of encountering such turbulence and explaining how it can be avoided..
