On 23 October 2020, a Bombardier DHC8-400 (JA845A) being operated by Oriental Air Bridge for All Nippon Airways on a scheduled domestic passenger flight from Fukuoka to Fukue as OC93 touched down at destination in day VMC with an excessive pitch attitude and the resulting runway contact caused substantial structural damage to the airframe but none of the 54 occupants were injured. The accident aircraft - the tailstrike damage was just aft of the rear door. [Reproduced from the Official Report]
