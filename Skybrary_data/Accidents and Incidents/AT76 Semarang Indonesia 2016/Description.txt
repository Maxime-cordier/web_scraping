On 25 December 2016, an ATR 72-600 (PK-WGW) being operated by Wings Abadi Airlines on a scheduled domestic passenger flight from Bandung to Semarang as WON1986 failed to make a normal touchdown after a stabilised approach in night VMC at destination and, after a rapid succession of five bounces, eventually stopped in a significantly damaged condition but with no injury to the 72 occupants. The aircraft where it stopped at the extreme edge of the paved surface. [Reproduced from the Official Report]
