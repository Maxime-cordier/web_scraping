Review of past statistics and studies show that pilot training and requirements focus on rejected takeoffs due to an engine failure. Studies and statistical information show that this accounts for less than 25% of the reasons for rejected takeoffs. Thus 75% of the reasons for the rejection of a takeoff are not trained for.. The Final Report of the Investigation: Rejected takeoff after the takeoff decision speed 'V1', B737-800, at Eindhoven Airport, 4 June 2010 was published on 23 June 2011. No Safety Recommendations were made.
