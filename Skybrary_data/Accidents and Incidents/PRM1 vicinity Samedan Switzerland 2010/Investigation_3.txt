One Safety Recommendation as a result of the Investigation as follows: that the Federal Office of Civil Aviation together with the Operator of Samedan airport should improve weather observation and transmission of important weather information so that approaching crews have all necessary information at their disposal for decision-making. The Final Report No. 2140 of the Investigation was completed on 23 April 2012 and approved by the Board on 12 June 2012
