The Investigation of this Serious Incident was delegated to the State of the Operator by China in accordance with ICAO Annex 13 and was commenced by the Japan Transport Safety Board (JTSB) on 22 August 2019. Relevant data was obtained from the aircraft EAFR. The 54 year-old Captain, who as acting as PF for the flight, was noted to have a total of 15,432 hours flying experience of which 4,523 hours were on type. The 34 year-old First Officer was noted to have a total of 4,331 hours flying experience of which 77 hours were on type.
