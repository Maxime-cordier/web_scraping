Three Safety Recommendations were made as follows: that great emphasis must be placed on the importance of exact compliance with instructions and clearances.. that the use of standard, concise and unequivocal aeronautical language is essential.. that (use of) the word "take-off" in the ATC clearance and (an) adequate time separation between (issue of) the ATC clearance and (issue of) the take off clearance is essential.. The Final Report in English of the Investigation was released by the Spanish Under Secretary of State for Civil Aviation in December 1978. The Final Report in Spanish was also released.
