The Cause of the accident was formally recorded as: “Continuation of a flight with a landing gear problem until the engines failed because of a lack of fuel” and Contributory Factors as: No or inadequate preventive measures by the manufacturer, certification authority and airline regarding previous publications of ICAO and the EU's DG VII relating to problems which had arisen and had been recorded following the introduction of aircraft equipped with advanced technology.. Anthropotechnical design shortcomings increasing the likelihood of misinterpretation of the FMS EFOB display..
