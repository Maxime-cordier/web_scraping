Civil Aviation should continuously monitor the activities of the Civil Aviation Authority of Nepal (CAAN) and airlines in order to augment the standard of aviation safety in Nepal.. that the Ministry of Culture, Tourism and Civil Aviation should coordinate with the responsible meteorological authorities to facilitate en-route weather information for STOL operations.. that the Department of Hydrology and Meteorology should develop and facilitate the provision of en-route weather information on various routes to STOL aerodromes.. that the Nepal Oil Corporation should develop a fuel supply and sampling system for all aircraft operated in Nepal.. The Final Report was submitted by the AAIC to the Ministry of Culture, Tourism and Civil Aviation on 31 July 2016 and was subsequently published in English in July 2017.
