Report contained three Safety Recommendations. Safety Recommendation 2010-014: It is recommended that the Federal Aviation Administration require that Cessna Aircraft Inc introduce a scheduled inspection of the Cessna Citation 1 throttle quadrant assembly to ensure the integrity of the riveted joints securing the fuel shut-off levers to the throttle levers. Safety Recommendation 2010-015: It is recommended that the Federal Aviation Administration require Cessna Aircraft Inc to amend the ‘EMERGENCY RESTART -TWO ENGINE’ checklist to emphasise the significance of only restarting one engine at a time. Safety Recommendation 2010-016: It is recommended that the International Civil Aviation Organisation adopt the proposals of its Flight Recorder Panel for the requirement to install flight recorders on turbine engine powered aeroplanes of a maximum certified takeoff mass of 5,700 kg or less.
