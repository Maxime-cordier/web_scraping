This incident occurred during the recovery of multiple military aircraft to Florennes, Belgium on completion of a NATO Tactical Leadership Programme (TLP) exercise over East Anglia. A formation of two foreign-operatyed Eurofighters not squawking failed to obtain the expected service from upon military ATC and then entered Class 'A' controlled airspace without clearance. The following is taken from the summary of information assembled by the UK Airprox Board (UKAB): While trying to establish proper contact with military ATC, the pair entered busy controlled airspace (CAS) near Heathrow.
