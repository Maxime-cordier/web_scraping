The formal statement of Cause from the Investigation was that the Serious Incident had been “due to a dangerous convergence of a cruising aircraft and an aircraft which climbed higher than its cleared flight level following the entry of an incorrect flight level into the flight management system". It was additionally concluded that the “absence of a system capable of detecting any discrepancy between the flight level cleared by ATC and that selected by the crew” had played a part. The Final Report was completed on 16 May 2013 and approved by the management of the Swiss AIB on 25 June 2013.
