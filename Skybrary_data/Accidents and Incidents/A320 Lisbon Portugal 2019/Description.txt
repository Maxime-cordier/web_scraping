On 16 September 2019, an Airbus A320 (G-EZWE) being operated by EasyJet on a scheduled international passenger flight from Lisbon to Manchester only became airborne 110 metres before the end of the 3705 metre-long runway 21 at Lisbon during its night takeoff in normal visibility. The remainder of the flight was without further event. A CCTV screenshot showing the aircraft just after reaching V1. The row of red runway end lights is visible to the right of the aircraft (in the yellow circle). [Reproduced from the Official Report]
