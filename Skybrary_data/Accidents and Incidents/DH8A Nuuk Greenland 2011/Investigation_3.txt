Safety Action taken by the Aircraft Operator as a result of the accident included the introduction of even stricter wind limits for BGGH and the provision of a graphical wind-rose for crew reference purposes. The Final Report was published in English translation on 2 May 2014 following initial publication in French on 10April 2014. No Safety Recommendations were made.
