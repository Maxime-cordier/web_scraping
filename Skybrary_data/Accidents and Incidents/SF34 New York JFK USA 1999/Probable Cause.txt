The Probable Cause of the accident was adopted by the NTSB on 4 December 2000 as: The pilot-in-command's failure to perform a missed approach as required by his company procedures.. It was also noted by the Board at this determination that “factors were the pilot-in-command's improper in-flight decisions, the pilot-in-command's failure to comply with FAA regulations and company procedures, inadequate crew coordination, and fatigue.”
