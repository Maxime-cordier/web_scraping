In respect of the ATS regulatory function of the ‘Inspection Transport and Public Works’ (IVW), the Investigation determined that “on this occasion the IVW did not completely fulfil its task as the supervisory agency” The Final Report of the Investigation: Air Proximity Between an Airbus A318 and a Boeing 737-900 was authorised for release on 29 February 2012. No Safety Recommendations were made as a result of the Investigation.
