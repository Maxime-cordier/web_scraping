On 4 March 2019, an Embraer 145 (N14171) being operated by CommutAir on behalf of United Express was on a scheduled domestic passenger flight from Newark to Presque Isle and attempting to land on runway 01 at destination in day IMC and in conditions equivalent to ILS minima. The first approach ended in a go around and the second ended in an off-runway crash landing which resulted in minor injuries to one of the pilots and two of the 28 passengers but also caused sufficient damage to the aircraft that it was declared a hull loss. The subsequently written off aircraft in its final resting position. [Reproduced from the Official Report]
