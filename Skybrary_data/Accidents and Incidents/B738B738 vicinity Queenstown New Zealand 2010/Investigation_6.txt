[016/12] The Final Report was approved for publication by the TAIC on 13 March 2012 and concluded by stating that the “key lessons” from the Investigation were: a pilot must not descend below the applicable instrument approach minimum descent altitude unless certain that the conditions are suitable for a landing. pilots must understand the operational assumptions in the design of instrument approach procedures, and how those assumptions determine the limits of safe manoeuvring. organisations that re-publish mutually important operational information from authoritative sources must ensure that the information is accurately reproduced so that all users interpret the information correctly and apply it consistently..
