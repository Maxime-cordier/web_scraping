On 24 February 2020, a Fairchild SA-227-DC Metro 23 (C-GIVB) being operated by Perimeter Aviation on a scheduled domestic passenger flight marketed under the wholly-owned ‘Bearskin Airlines’ brand from Dryden to Sioux Lookout as BLS344 and being used for First Officer Line Training began its takeoff on runway 12 at Dryden in normal day visibility but almost immediately directional control was lost and the aircraft departed the side of the runway and hit a frozen snow bank. The aircraft sustained substantial impact damage and one of the six passengers was seriously injured when propeller blade debris penetrated the fuselage.
