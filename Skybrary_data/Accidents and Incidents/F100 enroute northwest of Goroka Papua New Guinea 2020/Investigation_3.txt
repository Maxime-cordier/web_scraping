Two Safety Recommendations were made as a result of the Investigation as follows: that Air Niugini should ensure the use of its cabin defect report forms is reinforced for cabin crew to ensure that all cabin defects are recorded and reported in a timely manner to enable and facilitate maintenance actions.. that Air Niugini should ensure that in their Fokker 100 series aircraft which are not fitted with tape recorders or if the automatic announcement is inoperative or fails to work, the appropriate announcement will be made in accordance with the Operators Safety and Emergency Procedures Manual, when applicable.. The Final Report was approved for release on 22 November 2021 and subsequently published.
