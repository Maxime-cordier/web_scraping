The FAA issued an Airworthiness Directive (AD) applicable to all Boeing 737 series aircraft which required revisions to the Aircraft Flight Manual (AFM) within 60 days to include improved procedures for pre-flight setup of the cabin pressurisation system and improved procedures for interpreting and responding to the cabin altitude / configuration warning horn.. The Final Report of the Investigation was completed on 4 October 2006.
