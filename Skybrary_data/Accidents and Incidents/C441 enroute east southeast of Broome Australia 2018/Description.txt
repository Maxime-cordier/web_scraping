On 2 March 2018, a single-pilot Cessna 441 (VH-LBY) being operated by Skippers Aviation on a scheduled domestic passenger flight from Fitzroy Crossing to Broome in day VMC successively lost power on both engines due to fuel exhaustion. A MAYDAY was declared and since it was impossible to reach the destination or any other aerodrome, a forced landing was completed on the main highway in the area with no damage to the aircraft or injury to the 10 occupants. The aircraft secured in its final stopping position. [Reproduced from the Official Report]
