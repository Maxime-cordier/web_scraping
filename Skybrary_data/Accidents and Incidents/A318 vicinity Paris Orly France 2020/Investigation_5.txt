In addition, Air France persists with a stabilisation height which differs according to whether IMC (1000 feet) or VMC (500 feet) which is neither known nor, arguably, relevant. These deficiencies in the OFDM programme also compromise the effectiveness of the EBT approach to pilot training which Air France has embraced. No Safety Recommendations were made.
