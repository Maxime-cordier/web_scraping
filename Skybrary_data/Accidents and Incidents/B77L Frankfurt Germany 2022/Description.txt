On 4 September 2022, a Boeing 777F (D-AALU) being operated by Aerologic on an international scheduled cargo flight from Frankfurt as New York JFK with an augmented crew was taxiing as cleared towards its departure runway in unrestricted night visibility when its jet blast caused several empty airfreight containers to move towards personnel loading another aircraft and one fell to the ground in an attempt to avoid it and sustained a minor injury.
