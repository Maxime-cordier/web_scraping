Four Safety Recommendations were made as a result of the Investigation as follows: that the Aircraft Manufacturer and the (hydraulic system OEM) should introduce a unit to cancel the effect of spoiler stowage on the Automatic Brake System operation.. that the Aircraft Operator should add inspection of the hydraulic system components in the Before-Flight Check procedure.. that the Sudan Civil Aviation Authority should install facilities to record communication between all ATS Units and aircraft and between ATS Units at all Sudan airports.. that the Sudan Civil Aviation Authority should ensure than all airport ATC Tower locations should be situated in such a place as will give a clear view of the whole runway length. The Final Report of the Investigation was not published until June 2013.
