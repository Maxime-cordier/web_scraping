Airborne Collision Avoidance System (ACAS) operation requires that both aircraft - the interrogator and the target - are equipped with operating transponders. An aircraft equipped with ACAS will receive the following information depending on the type of transponder with which the target aircraft is equipped: Target aircraft equipped with:: Interrogator aircraft equipped with ACAS:: Mode 'A' transponder only. Below FL 155: Receives Traffic Advisory (TA) only (no altitude or trend arrow will be shown) Above FL 155: Will not show the target.
