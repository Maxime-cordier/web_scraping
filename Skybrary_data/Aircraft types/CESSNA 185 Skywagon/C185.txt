The Cessna 185, also known as the Skywagon, is a six-seat, single engined, general aviation light aircraft manufactured by Cessna. Wing span (metric): 10.92 m. Length (metric): 7.85 m. Height (metric): 2.36 m. Powerplant: 1 × Continental IO-520-D , 300 hp (220 kW). Engine Model(s): Continental O-520. 1 × Continental IO-520-D , 300 hp (220 kW)
