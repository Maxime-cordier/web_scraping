The Bell 505 Jet Ranger X is a single-engine glass cockpit light helicopter built by Bell Helicopter. Wing span (metric): 11.28 m. Length (metric): 12.95 m. Height (metric): 3.25 m. Powerplant: 1 x Safran HE Arrius 2R gas turbo-shaft, 505 shp (377 kW). 1 x Safran HE Arrius 2R gas turbo-shaft, 505 shp (377 kW)
