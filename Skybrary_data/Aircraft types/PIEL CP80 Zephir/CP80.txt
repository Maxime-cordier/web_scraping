The Piel CP.80 Zephir and Piel CP.801 are racing aircraft developed in France in the 1970s and marketed for homebuilding. They are compact, single-seat, single-engine monoplanes with low, cantilever wings. Wing span (metric): 5.30 m. Length (metric): 6.00 m. Height (metric): 1.70 m. Powerplant: 1 x Continental C90-8F, 67 kW (90 hp). Engine Model(s): Continental O-200. 1 x Continental C90-8F, 67 kW (90 hp)
