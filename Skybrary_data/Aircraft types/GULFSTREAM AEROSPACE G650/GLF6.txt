The Gulfstream G650 is a twin-engine business jet aircraft produced by Gulfstream Aerospace. The G650 is the company's largest and fastest business jet with a top speed of Mach 0.925. The G600 is the older variant of the G650 with a wingspan of 28.96m and length of 29.29m. Wing span (metric): 30.36 m. Length (metric): 30.41 m. Height (metric): 7.72 m. Powerplant: 2 x Rolls-Royce Deutschland BR725 turbofan, 71.6 kN each.. Engine Model(s): Rolls-Royce BR700. 2 x Rolls-Royce Deutschland BR725 turbofan, 71.6 kN each.
