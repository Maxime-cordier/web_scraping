Long to ultra long range wide-body airliner. First flight in April 2001. First delivery to Virgin Atlantic in 2002. Stretched development of A340-500. Total of 116 aircraft ordered, 60 are in operation (August 2006). Wing span (metric): 63.45 m. Length (metric): 75.36 m. Height (metric): 17.22 m. Powerplant: 4 x R-R Trent 556 (249kN) turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Rolls-Royce Trent 500. 4 x R-R Trent 556 (249kN) turbofans.
