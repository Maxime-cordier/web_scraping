Narrow body short to medium range airliner, based on (and intended to replace) the B739 with a seating capacity of 220 and range up to 3550 NM. First flight 2017, certified in 2018. This is the second aircraft of the Boeing 737 MAX family which also includes the MAX 7, BOEING 737 MAX 8 and MAX 10. The MAX series features more efficient engines, a new type of winglets and some airframe modifications. Wing span (metric): 35.90 m. Length (metric): 42.16 m. Height (metric): 12.30 m. Powerplant: 2 x CFM International LEAP-1B (130 kN) turbofans. Engine Model(s): CFM International LEAP. 2 x CFM International LEAP-1B (130 kN) turbofans
