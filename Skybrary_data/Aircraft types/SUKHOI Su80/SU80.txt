The Sukhoi Su-80 is a Russian twin-turboprop, twin-boom Short Take-Off and Landing (STOL) transport aircraft. Wing span (metric): 23.18 m. Length (metric): 18.26 m. Height (metric): 5.74 m. Powerplant: 2 x General Electric CT7-9B turboprop, 1,305 kW (1,750 hp) each.. Engine Model(s): General Electric CT7. 2 x General Electric CT7-9B turboprop, 1,305 kW (1,750 hp) each.
