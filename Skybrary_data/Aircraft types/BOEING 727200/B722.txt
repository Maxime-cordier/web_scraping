Short to medium range airliner. In service since 1968. Most numerous member of BOEING 727 family. 727-200S converted quiet version with JT8D-217S engines. Production ceased in 1984. 1240 aircraft of 727-200 were built. Approx. 940 remain in commercial service in 1999. Wing span (metric): 32.90 m. Length (metric): 46.70 m. Height (metric): 10.40 m.
