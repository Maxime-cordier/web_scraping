Heavylift freighter. In service since 1987. Antonovs heavylift freighter development to replace the An-22. An-124 configuration is similar to the LOCKHEAD C-5 Galaxy. Only a limited number built and remain in service. Range with max payload is 3200nm, with 120t the range is 5200nm and with 40t range is 11900nm. Wing span (metric): 73.30 m. Length (metric): 69.10 m. Height (metric): 20.78 m. Powerplant: 4 x ZMKB Progress D-18T (229 kN) turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Progress D-18T. 4 x ZMKB Progress D-18T (229 kN) turbofans.
