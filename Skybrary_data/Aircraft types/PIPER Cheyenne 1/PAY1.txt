Originally, the aircraft was an upgraded version of the Pressurized Navajo equipped with two Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6A-28 turboprop engines. Later, the aircraft was further refined and developed, including aerodynamic improvements and fuselage extensions. The PA-31T led to the development of the PA-42 Cheyenne III and IV. Wing span (metric): 13.01 m. Length (metric): 10.57 m. Height (metric): 3.89 m. Powerplant: 2 x 620 SHP P&W PT6A-28 turboprops with 3 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6. 2 x 620 SHP P&W PT6A-28 turboprops with 3 blade propellers.
