The Bombardier Challenger 300 is a super-mid-sized jet capable of traversing transcontinental distances. It is not developmentally related to the similarly-named Challenger 600-series, or the 600-derived Challenger 800-series. Wing span (metric): 18.40 m. Length (metric): 20.90 m. Height (metric): 6.10 m. Powerplant: 2 x Honeywell HTF7000 turbofans, 30.4 kN thrust.. Engine Model(s): Honeywell HTF7000. 2 x Honeywell HTF7000 turbofans, 30.4 kN thrust.
