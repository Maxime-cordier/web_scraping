Light weight multi-role fighter. In service since 1996. SAABs sixth fighter development for Royal Swedish Air Force to replace the Viggen. Two seat version SK 39B (first flight 22. Nov. 1996). First export customer is South Africa. Wing span (metric): 8.00 m. Length (metric): 14.10 m. Height (metric): 4.50 m. Powerplant: 1 x 80,5 kN Volvo Flygmotor RM12 (F404-GE-400) afterburning turbofan.. Engine Model(s): Volvo RM12. 1 x 80,5 kN Volvo Flygmotor RM12 (F404-GE-400) afterburning turbofan.
