The Boeing 747 Large Cargo Freighter (LCF), Dreamlifter, is a wide-body cargo aircraft. Constructed by drastic modifications to an existing Boeing 747-400, the Dreamlifter is used exclusively for transporting aircraft parts to Boeing from suppliers around the world. The BLCF is member of the B747 family of aircraft. Wing span (metric): 64.40 m. Length (metric): 71.68 m. Height (metric): 21.54 m. Powerplant: 4 x 282 kN PW 4062 turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney PW4000. 4 x 282 kN PW 4062 turbofans.
