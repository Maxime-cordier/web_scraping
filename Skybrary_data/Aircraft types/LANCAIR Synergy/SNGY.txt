The Synergy is a fixed-gear, two-place, side-by-side, Lycoming IO-390-powered design similar in appearance to the Lancair Legacy high-performance kit-built aircraft, but with a larger wing and tail. Wing span (metric): 8.60 m. Length (metric): 6.70 m. Powerplant: 1 x Lycoming IO-390. Engine Model(s): Lycoming IO-390. 1 x Lycoming IO-390
