Long range, high capacity wide-body airliner. In service since 1989. The first 747 which did not require a flight engineer as part of the flight crew and as such the 'second generation' of the B747 family. Wing span (metric): 64.40 m. Length (metric): 70.60 m. Height (metric): 19.40 m. Powerplant: 4 x R-R RB211-524 G/H (258 kN) or 4 x P&W PW4056 (252.4 kN) or 4 x GE CF6-80C2B4 (257.5 kN) turbofans, and more.. Engine Model(s): General Electric CF6. 4 x R-R RB211-524 G/H (258 kN) or 4 x P&W PW4056 (252.4 kN) or 4 x GE CF6-80C2B4 (257.5 kN) turbofans, and more.
