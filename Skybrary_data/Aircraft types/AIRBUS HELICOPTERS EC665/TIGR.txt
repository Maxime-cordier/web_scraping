The Eurocopter Tiger (company designation EC 665) is an attack helicopter manufactured by Eurocopter. In Germany it is known as the Tiger; in France and Spain it is called the Tigre. Wing span (metric): 13.00 m. Length (metric): 14.80 m. Height (metric): 3.83 m. Powerplant: 2 x Rolls-Royce/Turboméca/MTU MTR390 turboshafts, 873 kW (1,170 shp) each.. Engine Model(s): MTR390. 2 x Rolls-Royce/Turboméca/MTU MTR390 turboshafts, 873 kW (1,170 shp) each.
