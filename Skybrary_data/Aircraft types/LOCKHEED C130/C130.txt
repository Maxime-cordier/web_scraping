Military tactical and multi-role transporter with civil cargo versions. In service since 1955. Exists in many variants both civil and military. The L-100/382 is a stretched civil version. Performance figures are for the C-130H. The C-130J is a modernised military version with new engines and electronics. Wing span (metric): 40.40 m. Length (metric): 29.80 m. Height (metric): 11.80 m. Powerplant: 4 x Allison T56-A15 (4.508 SHP) turboprops with 4 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Allison T56. 4 x Allison T56-A15 (4.508 SHP) turboprops with 4 blade propellers.
