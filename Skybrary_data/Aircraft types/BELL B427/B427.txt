The Bell Model 427 is a twin-engine civil utility helicopter, a new design derived from on the Bell 407. It is scheduled to be replaced by the lengthened Bell 429. Wing span (metric): 11.28 m. Length (metric): 10.94 m. Height (metric): 3.49 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PW207D turboshaft, 710 hp (529 kW) each.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PW200. 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PW207D turboshaft, 710 hp (529 kW) each.
