The Eurocopter EC130 is a single engine light helicopter developed from the AS350 Ecureuil airframe. Wing span (metric): 10.69 m. Length (metric): 10.68 m. Height (metric): 3.34 m. Powerplant: 1 x Turboméca Arriel 2B1 turboshaft, 632 kW (847 shp).. Engine Model(s): Turboméca Arriel. 1 x Turboméca Arriel 2B1 turboshaft, 632 kW (847 shp).
