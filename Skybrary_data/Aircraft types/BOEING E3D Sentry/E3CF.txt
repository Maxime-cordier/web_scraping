Airborne warning and control system (AWACS) aircraft. In service since 1986 (E3D/F since 1992). Developed from E-3A standard Sentry for Saudi Arabia (E3/A) Royal Air Force (E-3D) and French Air Force (E3-F) with more powerful CFM engines and refueling probe. E-3D with wingtip mounted ESM pods. Operation radius for 10 hours patrol without refuelling. Wing span (metric): 44.40 m. Length (metric): 46.60 m. Height (metric): 12.60 m. Powerplant: 4 x 106.8 kN CFM 56-2A-2/3 turbofans.. 4 x 106.8 kN CFM 56-2A-2/3 turbofans.
