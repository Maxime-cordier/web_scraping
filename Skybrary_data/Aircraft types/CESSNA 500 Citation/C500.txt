Light corporate jet. In service since 1971. Initial version of a large family of Business jets. In production between 1971 and 1985. Replaced by CESSNA 525 CitationJet in 1985. Wing span (metric): 14.40 m. Length (metric): 13.30 m. Height (metric): 4.40 m. Powerplant: 2 x 9.98 kN P&W JT15D-1 turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada JT15D. 2 x 9.98 kN P&W JT15D-1 turbofans.
