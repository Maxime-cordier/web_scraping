The Khrunichev T-411 Aist is a Russian light utility monoplane designed by the Khrunichev design bureau. Marketed in the United States as the Aeroprogress T-411 Wolverine powered by a Continental TSIO-550-B. Wing span (metric): 13.02 m. Length (metric): 9.50 m. Height (metric): 2.60 m. Powerplant: 1 x 360HP Vedeneyev M14P radial piston engine.. Engine Model(s): Vedeneyev M14P. 1 x 360HP Vedeneyev M14P radial piston engine.
