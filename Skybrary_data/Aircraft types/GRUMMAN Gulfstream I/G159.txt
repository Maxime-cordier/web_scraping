Grumman Gulfstream I (company designation G-159) is a twin turboprop business aircraft. It first flew on August 14, 1958. Wing span (metric): 23.93 m. Length (metric): 19.43 m. Height (metric): 6.93 m. Powerplant: 2 x Rolls-Royce Dart 529 turboprop, 2,190 shp (1,630 kW) each.. Engine Model(s): Rolls-Royce Dart. 2 x Rolls-Royce Dart 529 turboprop, 2,190 shp (1,630 kW) each.
