The P68 is an Italian six-seat, twin-engined, high-wing monoplane built by Partenavia and later Vulcanair. The P68 Observer, which was an Italian/German development, has a transparent nose for use in police work and observation duties. Wing span (metric): 12.00 m. Length (metric): 9.40 m.
