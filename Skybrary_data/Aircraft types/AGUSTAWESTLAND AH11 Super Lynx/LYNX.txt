The Westland Lynx is a British multi-purpose military helicopter designed and built by Westland Helicopters at its factory in Yeovil. The helicopter is now produced and marketed by AgustaWestland. The AgustaWestland AW159 Lynx Wildcat (previously called the Future Lynx) is an improved version of the Westland Super Lynx military helicopter. Wing span (metric): 12.80 m. Length (metric): 15.24 m. Height (metric): 3.79 m. Powerplant: 2 x Rolls-Royce Gem turboshaft, 835 kW each.. Engine Model(s): LHTEC T800. 2 x Rolls-Royce Gem turboshaft, 835 kW each.
