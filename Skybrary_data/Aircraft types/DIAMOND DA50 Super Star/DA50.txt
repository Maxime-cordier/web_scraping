The Diamond DA50 Super Star and DA50 Magnum are five-place single-engine composite aircraft designed by Diamond Aircraft Industries. Introduced in 2006, the project was later put on hold and the current status of the product is unknown. Wing span (metric): 11.68 m. Length (metric): 8.81 m. Height (metric): 2.21 m. Powerplant: 1 x Teledyne Continental TSIOF-550J six cylinder, four-stroke, piston aircraft engine, 350 hp (260 kW).
