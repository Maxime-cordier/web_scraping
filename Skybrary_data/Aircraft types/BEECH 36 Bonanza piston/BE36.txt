High performance light aircraft. In service since 1968 (turbocharged since 1979). Stretched, more powerful development of BEECH 33 Debonair. More than 3700 BEECH A36 and 600 turbocharged BEECH 36TC were built by Beechcraft. Production continued by RAYTHEON. Wing span (metric): 8.40 m. Length (metric): 8.10 m. Height (metric): 2.60 m. Powerplant: 1 x 300 HP Continental IO-550-B piston or. Engine Model(s): Continental IO-550. 1 x 300 HP Continental IO-550-B piston or
