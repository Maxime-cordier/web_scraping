Medium to long range wide-body airliner. In service since 1986 (ER since 1989). Stretched version of B767-200. Exists in an ER (extended range) version for Extended Range Twin Engine Operation. The B763 is member of the B767 family of aircraft. Wing span (metric): 47.60 m. Length (metric): 54.90 m. Height (metric): 15.90 m. Powerplant: 2 x P&W PW4062 (281.6 kN) or 2 x GE CF6-80C2B7F (276.2 kN) or 2 x RR RB211-524H (264.7 kN). Engine Model(s): General Electric CF6. 2 x P&W PW4062 (281.6 kN) or 2 x GE CF6-80C2B7F (276.2 kN) or 2 x RR RB211-524H (264.7 kN)
