Regional airliner. In service since 1992. Airliner development of CL-601 Challenger business jet. Initially 100 series replaced by 200 series in 1993. Canadair is now part of the Bombardier Group. The CRJ1 is a member of the CRJ family. The aircraft carries the same Type Certificate as the Bombardier Regional Jet CRJ-200. Wing span (metric): 21.21 m. Length (metric): 26.80 m. Height (metric): 6.30 m. Powerplant: 2 x 41 KN GE CF34-3A1 turbofans.. Engine Model(s): General Electric CF34. 2 x 41 KN GE CF34-3A1 turbofans.
