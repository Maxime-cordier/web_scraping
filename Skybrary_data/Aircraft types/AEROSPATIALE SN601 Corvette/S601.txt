Light corporate jet. In service since 1974. Light corporate jet development for multi roles. Production ceased in 1977 after 39 aircraft were built. 33 of the Corvettes remain in service by corporate operators in Europe. Wing span (metric): 12.90 m. Length (metric): 13.80 m.
