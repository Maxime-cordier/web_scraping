Long range, high capacity wide-body airliner. In service since 1976. Shortened version of the B747-200 with a better performance (SP for Special Performance), optimised for African and Pacific long range non-stop services. The B74S is member of the B747 family of aircraft. Wing span (metric): 59.60 m. Length (metric): 56.30 m. Height (metric): 19.40 m. Powerplant: 4 x P&W JT9D-1AW (218.4 kN) or 4 x GE CF6-45A2 (206.8 kN) or 4 x R-R RB 211-524B (222.8 kN) or 4 x R-R RB 211-524D4 (230 kN) turbofans.. Engine Model(s): General Electric CF6. 4 x P&W JT9D-1AW (218.4 kN) or 4 x GE CF6-45A2 (206.8 kN) or 4 x R-R RB 211-524B (222.8 kN) or 4 x R-R RB 211-524D4 (230 kN) turbofans.
