The Junkers Ju 52/3m (nicknamed Tante Ju ("Aunt Ju") and Iron Annie) is a transport aircraft that was designed and manufactured by German aviation company Junkers. The Ju 52 was in production between 1931 and 1952. Wing span (metric): 29.25 m. Length (metric): 18.90 m. Height (metric): 4.50 m.
