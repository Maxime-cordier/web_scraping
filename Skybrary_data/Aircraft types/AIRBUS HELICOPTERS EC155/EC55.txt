The Eurocopter EC155 is a long-range medium-lift passenger transport helicopter developed by Eurocopter from the Dauphin family for civil aviation use. It is a twin-engined aircraft and can carry up to 13 passengers along with 1 or 2 crew, depending on customer configuration. The helicopter is marketed for passenger transport, offshore support, VIP corporate transport and casualty transport duties. Wing span (metric): 12.60 m. Length (metric): 14.30 m. Height (metric): 4.35 m. Powerplant: 2 x Turboméca Arriel 2C2 turboshafts, 697 kW (943 shp) each.. Engine Model(s): Turboméca Arriel. 2 x Turboméca Arriel 2C2 turboshafts, 697 kW (943 shp) each.
