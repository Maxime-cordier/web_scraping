Utility twin aircraft. In service since 1969. Less powerful, light and low cost model of CESSNA 421 Golden Eagle. Features wing of CESSNA 402 and fuselage of CESSNA 421. Improved version CESSNA 414A Chancellor (appeared in 1978) with bonded wet wing, without tip tanks. Remained in production until 1985. Wing span (metric): 12.50 m. Length (metric): 10.30 m. Height (metric): 3.60 m. Powerplant: 2 x 310 HP Continental TSIO-520-NB turbocharged piston engines with 3 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Continental O-520. 2 x 310 HP Continental TSIO-520-NB turbocharged piston engines with 3 blade propellers.
