The AIDC F-CK-1 Ching-kuo is an technologically advanced air superiority jet fighter named after the late President of the Republic of China (Taiwan) Chiang Ching-kuo. It entered active service with the Republic of China (Taiwan) Air Force in 1994, and 131 production aircraft had been manufactured by 1999. Wing span (metric): 9.46 m. Length (metric): 14.21 m. Height (metric): 4.42 m. Powerplant: 2 x Honeywell F125-70.. Engine Model(s): Honeywell F124. 2 x Honeywell F125-70.
