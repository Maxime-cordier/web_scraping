Turboprop trainer aircraft with tandem cockpit. Manufactured by Pilatus Aircraft of Switzerland. First flight in 2002, type certification in 2004. Can be used for various purposes, including simulation, basic, advanced and fighter lead-in training. Wing span (metric): 9.11 m. Length (metric): 11.22 m. Height (metric): 3.75 m. Powerplant: 1 x PT6A-68B turboprop (1600 shp). Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6. 1 x PT6A-68B turboprop (1600 shp)
