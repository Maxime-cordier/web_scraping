Further lengthened version of the DC-9-30 entered service in March 1968. With a 2m longer fuselage, accommodation was up to 125 passengers. The -40 was fitted with Pratt & Whitney engines of between 64 and 71 kN. A total of 71 were produced. The DC94 is member of the DC-9 family of aircraft. Wing span (metric): 28.47 m. Length (metric): 38.28 m. Height (metric): 8.38 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney JT8D-7, -9, -11 or -15 (68.9 kN) thrust each.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney JT8D. 2 x Pratt & Whitney JT8D-7, -9, -11 or -15 (68.9 kN) thrust each.
