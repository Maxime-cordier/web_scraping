Short range airliner. In service since 1969. The most used member of first generation of B737 family. Stretched version of the orginal 737-100. Production ceased in 1988. 1144 aircraft of 737-200 were built, approx. 900 remain in service in 1999. Mil. type: CT-43 and VC96. For more information, see Boeing's B737 family specifications.
