Short to medium range airliner. In service since 1980. It has longer range and greater maximum take off mass (MTOW) than the other members of the MD-80 series. Produced until December 1999. Wing span (metric): 32.78 m. Length (metric): 45.06 m. Height (metric): 9.05 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney JT8D-219 (93,41 kN).. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney JT8D. 2 x Pratt & Whitney JT8D-219 (93,41 kN).
