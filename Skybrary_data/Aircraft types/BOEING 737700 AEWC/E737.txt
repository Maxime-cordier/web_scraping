The Boeing 737 AEW&C is a twin-engine airborne early warning and control aircraft. It is lighter than the 707-based Boeing E-3 Sentry, and mounts a fixed, electronically scanned, rather than a rotating, radar antenna. Wing span (metric): 35.80 m. Length (metric): 33.60 m. Height (metric): 12.50 m. Powerplant: 2 x CFM International CFM56-7B27A turbofans, 118 kN each.. Engine Model(s): CFM International CFM56. 2 x CFM International CFM56-7B27A turbofans, 118 kN each.
