Executive transport aircraft. In service since 1980. Turbo-prop powered version of CESSNA 421 Golden Eagle. Cessna renamed the Corsair to Conquest 1 and the Conquest to Conquest 2 in 1983. Production ceased in 1986. Wing span (metric): 13.50 m. Length (metric): 10.90 m. Height (metric): 3.90 m. Powerplant: 2 x 450 SHP PW PT6A-112 turboprops with 3 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6. 2 x 450 SHP PW PT6A-112 turboprops with 3 blade propellers.
