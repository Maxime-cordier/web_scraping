The Bell 222 is a twin-engined light helicopter built by Bell Helicopter. The Bell 230 is an improved development with different engines and other minor changes. Wing span (metric): 12.20 m. Length (metric): 12.85 m. Height (metric): 3.56 m. Powerplant: 2 x Lycoming LTS-101-650C-3, 618 hp (461 kW) each.. Engine Model(s): Lycoming LTS101. 2 x Lycoming LTS-101-650C-3, 618 hp (461 kW) each.
