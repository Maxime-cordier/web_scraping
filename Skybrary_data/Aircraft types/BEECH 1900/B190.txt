The Beechcraft 1900 is a 19-passenger, pressurized twin-engine turboprop fixed-wing aircraft manufactured by the Beechcraft Division of the Raytheon Company (now Textron Aviation).The 1900 first flew on September 3, 1982.Raytheon ended production of the Beechcraft 1900 in October 2002. Wing span (metric): 17.70 m. Length (metric): 17.60 m. Height (metric): 4.70 m. Powerplant: 1900C: 2 x 1.100 SHP P&W PT6A - 65B turboprops with 4 blade propellers..
