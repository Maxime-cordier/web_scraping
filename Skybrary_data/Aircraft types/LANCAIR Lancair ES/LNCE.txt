The Lancair ES is an American amateur-built aircraft that was designed and produced by Lancair. While it was in production the aircraft was supplied in the form of a kit for amateur construction. Wing span (metric): 10.82 m. Length (metric): 7.62 m. Height (metric): 2.40 m. Powerplant: 1 x Continental IO-550 six cylinder, air-cooled, four stroke aircraft engine, 310 hp..
