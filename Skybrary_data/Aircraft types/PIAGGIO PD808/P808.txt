Commuter airliner and utility transport aircraft. First prototype flew in August 1965. Operated by Italian Air Force as transport and EW (Electronic Warfare) aircraft. Wing span (metric): 13.20 m. Length (metric): 12.80 m. Height (metric): 4.50 m. Powerplant: 2 x 15.24 kN R-R (Bristol Siddeley) Viper 525 turbojets.. Engine Model(s): Rolls-Royce Viper. 2 x 15.24 kN R-R (Bristol Siddeley) Viper 525 turbojets.
