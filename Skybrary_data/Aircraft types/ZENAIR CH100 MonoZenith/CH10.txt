The Zenair Mono-Z CH 100 is a single-seat, single-engined Canadian light aircraft of the 1970s. It is a smaller version of the Zenair CH 200 with a less powerful engine, which was sold as a homebuilt aircraft by Zenair. Wing span (metric): 6.71 m. Length (metric): 5.94 m. Height (metric): 1.98 m. Powerplant: 1 x Volkswagen, 65 hp.. 1 x Volkswagen, 65 hp.
