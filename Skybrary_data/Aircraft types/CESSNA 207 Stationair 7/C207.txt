Utility light aircraft. In service since 1969. Stretched fuselage version of C206. Known as Stationair 7 from 1978, was replaced by 207A Stationair 8 from 1979. The Turbine 207 uses an Allison 250-C20S turboprop engine which has improved the performance significantly. Note: Has 4 side windows. Wing span (metric): 10.92 m. Length (metric): 8.62 m. Height (metric): 2.92 m. Powerplant: 1 x 300 HP Continental IO-520-F or. Engine Model(s): Continental O-520. 1 x 300 HP Continental IO-520-F or
