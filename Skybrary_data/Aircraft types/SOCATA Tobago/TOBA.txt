Light aircraft. In service since 1979. Basic member of SOCATAs caribbean TB single engine aircraft series to replace the Rallye family. TB 10 Tobago and TB-200 Tobago XL are similar to TB-9 Tampico (TAMP) but with improved gear, higher weight and more powerful engine. Wing span (metric): 9.80 m. Length (metric): 7.70 m. Height (metric): 3.00 m. Powerplant: 1 x 180 HP Lycoming O-360-A1AD piston engine with 2 blade propeller.. Engine Model(s): Lycoming O-360. 1 x 180 HP Lycoming O-360-A1AD piston engine with 2 blade propeller.
