High performance light aircraft. In service since 1956 (182S since 1997). CESSNA 182S new more powerful re-introduced development of CESSNA 182 with new interior and avionics panel. Production of the basic Cessna 182 ended in 1985 but the 182S was introduced in 1997. Note: Has 2 side windows and flat rear window. Wing span (metric): 11.00 m. Length (metric): 8.60 m. Height (metric): 2.80 m. Powerplant: 1 x 230 Hp Lycoming IO-540-AB1A5 piston engine with 2 blade propeller.. Engine Model(s): Lycoming O-540. 1 x 230 Hp Lycoming IO-540-AB1A5 piston engine with 2 blade propeller.
