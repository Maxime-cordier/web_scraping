The Grob G-115 is an advanced general aviation fixed-wing aircraft, primarily used for flight training. It is built in Germany by Grob Aircraft (Grob Aerospace before January 2009). The E variant with a 3-blade variable pitch propeller is in Royal Air Force service as an elementary flying trainer. Wing span (metric): 10.00 m. Length (metric): 7.54 m. Height (metric): 2.40 m. Powerplant: 1 x Lycoming O-360-A1B6 4-cylinder air-cooled piston engine, 139 kW (180 hp).. Engine Model(s): Lycoming O-360. 1 x Lycoming O-360-A1B6 4-cylinder air-cooled piston engine, 139 kW (180 hp).
