The Cirrus SR22 is a single-engine, originally four and later five-seat, composite aircraft, built by Cirrus Aircraft starting in 2001. It is a more powerful version of the Cirrus CIRRUS SR-20, with a larger wing, higher fuel capacity, and a has fixed (non-retractable) landing gear. Wing span (metric): 11.68 m. Length (metric): 7.92 m. Height (metric): 2.72 m. Powerplant: 1 x Continental IO-550-N Six cylinder horizontally opposed aircraft engine, 310 hp (230 kW).. Engine Model(s): Continental IO-550. 1 x Continental IO-550-N Six cylinder horizontally opposed aircraft engine, 310 hp (230 kW).
