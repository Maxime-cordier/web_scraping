The Beriev Be-12 Tchaika (Seagull, NATO reporting name: Mail) is a Soviet twin-turboprop-powered amphibious anti-submarine and maritime patrol aircraft. Wing span (metric): 29.84 m. Length (metric): 30.11 m. Height (metric): 7.94 m. Powerplant: 2 x Ivchenko-Progress AI-20D turboprops, 3,864 kW (5,180 hp) each.. Engine Model(s): Ivchenko AI-20. 2 x Ivchenko-Progress AI-20D turboprops, 3,864 kW (5,180 hp) each.
