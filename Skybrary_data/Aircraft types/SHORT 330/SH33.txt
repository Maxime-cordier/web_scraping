Regional airliner and utility freighter. In service since 1975. Improved, stretched development of SC-7 Skyvan. More powerful development 330-200. Various freighter versions were built including the Sherpa with a rear loading ramp. Military version C-23 for US Air Force and Army and 330-UT for Thailands Army. Wing span (metric): 22.80 m. Length (metric): 17.70 m. Height (metric): 5.00 m. Powerplant: 100: 2 x 1.156 SHP P&W PT6A-45B Turboprops with 5 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6. 100: 2 x 1.156 SHP P&W PT6A-45B Turboprops with 5 blade propellers.
