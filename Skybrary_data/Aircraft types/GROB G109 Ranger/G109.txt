The Grob G109 is a two-seat self-launching motor glider in which the pilot and passenger or student sit side by side. Wing span (metric): 17.40 m. Length (metric): 8.10 m. Height (metric): 1.70 m. Powerplant: 1 x Grob 2500E1 4-cylinder air-cooled piston engine, 95 shp (71 kW). 1 x Grob 2500E1 4-cylinder air-cooled piston engine, 95 shp (71 kW)
