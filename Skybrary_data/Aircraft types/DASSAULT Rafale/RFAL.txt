The Dassault Rafale is a French twin-engined delta-wing agile multi-role 4.5th-generation jet fighter aircraft designed and built by Dassault Aviation. Introduced in 2000, the Rafale is being produced both for land-based use with the French Air Force and for carrier-based operations with the French Navy. Wing span (metric): 10.80 m. Length (metric): 15.27 m. Height (metric): 5.34 m. Powerplant: 2 x Snecma M88-2 turbofans.. Engine Model(s): SNECMA M88. 2 x Snecma M88-2 turbofans.
