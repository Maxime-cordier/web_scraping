The Gulfstream II is an American twin engine business jet built by Gulfstream Aerospace, and its US military designation is C-11 Gulfstream II. It has been succeeded by the C-20 Gulfstream III. Wing span (metric): 20.98 m. Length (metric): 24.36 m. Height (metric): 7.47 m. Powerplant: 2 x Rolls-Royce Spey 511-8 turbofan, 11,400 lbf (51 kN) each.. Engine Model(s): Rolls-Royce Spey. 2 x Rolls-Royce Spey 511-8 turbofan, 11,400 lbf (51 kN) each.
