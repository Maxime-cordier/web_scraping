The Diamond DA20 is a two-seat tricycle gear general aviation aircraft designed for flight training. In addition to its role as a civil and military training aircraft, it is also used for personal flying by pilot-owners. Wing span (metric): 10.87 m. Length (metric): 7.16 m. Height (metric): 2.18 m. Powerplant: 1 x Continental IO-240-B. Engine Model(s): Continental O-240. 1 x Continental IO-240-B
