The Bell 47 is a two-bladed, single engine, light helicopter manufactured by Bell Helicopter. The 47G introduced in 1953, can be recognized by the full bubble canopy, exposed welded-tube tail boom, saddle fuel tanks, and skid landing gear. Wing span (metric): 11.32 m. Length (metric): 9.63 m. Height (metric): 2.83 m. Powerplant: 1 x Lycoming TVO-435-F1A flat, six-cylinder, reciprocating engine, 280 hp (210 kW).
