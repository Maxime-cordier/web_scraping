Regional airliner. In service since 1999. Shortened 35 seats development of the ERJ-145 (re-named from EMB-145). Built as ER, standard version and LR slightly more powerful extended range version with additional fuel tank. The ERJ-140 is a stretched version with increased seating capacity and range. The E135 is a member of the ERJ Family of aircraft. Wing span (metric): 20.04 m. Length (metric): 26.33 m. Height (metric): 6.76 m. Powerplant: ER: 2 x 31,3 kN Allison AE-3007A3 turbofans.
