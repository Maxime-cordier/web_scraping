Strategic long range bomber. In service since 1981. Production ended with the 100th aircraft in Jan.1988. In 1990 the USAF brought up the B-1 as B-1B to a common avionics standard. The USAF named the B-1B Lancer. Wing span (metric): 41.70 m. Length (metric): 44.80 m. Height (metric): 10.40 m. Powerplant: 4 x GE F101 - GE - 102 (136.9 kN) afterburning turbofans.. Engine Model(s): General Electric F101. 4 x GE F101 - GE - 102 (136.9 kN) afterburning turbofans.
