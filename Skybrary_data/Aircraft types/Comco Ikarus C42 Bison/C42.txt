The Ikarus C42 is a two-seat, fixed tricycle gear, general aviation microlight aircraft, manufactured in Germany by Comco Ikarus. It is used primarily for flight training, touring and personal flying. Wing span (metric): 9.45 m. Length (metric): 6.25 m. Height (metric): 2.24 m. Powerplant: 1 x Rotax 912ULS flat-4 engine, 100 hp (75 kW). Engine Model(s): Rotax 912. 1 x Rotax 912ULS flat-4 engine, 100 hp (75 kW)
