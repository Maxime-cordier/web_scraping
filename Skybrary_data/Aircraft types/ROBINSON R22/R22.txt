The Robinson R22 is a two-bladed, single-engine light utility helicopter manufactured by Robinson Helicopter. The two-seat R22 was designed in 1973 by Frank Robinson and has been in production since 1979. Wing span (metric): 7.70 m. Length (metric): 8.70 m. Height (metric): 2.70 m. Powerplant: 1× Lycoming O-320-A2B or -A2C flat 4 piston engine, 124 hp (93 kW).. 1× Lycoming O-320-A2B or -A2C flat 4 piston engine, 124 hp (93 kW).
