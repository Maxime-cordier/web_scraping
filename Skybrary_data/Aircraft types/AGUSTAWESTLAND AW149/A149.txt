The AgustaWestland AW149 is a medium-lift military helicopter being developed by AgustaWestland. Wing span (metric): 14.60 m. Length (metric): 17.57 m. Height (metric): 5.06 m. Powerplant: 2 x General Electric CT7-2E1 turboshaft engine, 1,477 kW each.. Engine Model(s): General Electric CT7. 2 x General Electric CT7-2E1 turboshaft engine, 1,477 kW each.
