Light corporate jet. In service since 2000 (FAA certification in April, first delivery in November). Stretched development of Citation Jet replacement CJ1 with extra two cabin windows. Wing span (metric): 15.10 m. Length (metric): 14.30 m. Height (metric): 4.20 m. Powerplant: 2 x 10,7 kN Williams FJ44-2C turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Williams FJ44. 2 x 10,7 kN Williams FJ44-2C turbofans.
