Medium size corporate jet. First flight in 1976. More powerful turbofan development of IAI 1123 Westwind. In 1980, the basic IAI 1124 has been led to the 1124A Westwind 2 with improved performance, modified wing section and winglets. Production ceased in 1987 in favour of the IAI 1125 Astra. Wing span (metric): 13.70 m. Length (metric): 15.90 m. Height (metric): 4.80 m. Powerplant: 2 x 16.58 kN Garrett TFE731-3 turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Garrett TFE731. 2 x 16.58 kN Garrett TFE731-3 turbofans.
