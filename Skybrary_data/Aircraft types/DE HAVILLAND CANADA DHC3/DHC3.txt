The de Havilland Canada DHC-3 Otter is a single-engined, high-wing, propeller-driven, Short Take-Off and Landing (STOL) aircraft developed by de Havilland Canada. Wing span (metric): 17.69 m. Length (metric): 12.80 m. Height (metric): 3.83 m. Powerplant: 1 x Pratt & Whitney R-1340-S1H1-G Wasp 9-cylinder air-cooled radial engine, 600 hp (448 kW).. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney R-1340. 1 x Pratt & Whitney R-1340-S1H1-G Wasp 9-cylinder air-cooled radial engine, 600 hp (448 kW).
