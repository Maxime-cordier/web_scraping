Designed to satisfy a request for improved short field performance by using the more powerful engines and improved wings of the -30 combined with the shorter fuselage used in the -10. Ten Series 20 aircraft were produced, all of them Model -21. The DC92 is member of the DC-9 family of aircraft. Wing span (metric): 27.25 m. Length (metric): 31.82 m. Height (metric): 8.38 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney JT8D-11 (66.7 kN) thrust each.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney JT8D. 2 x Pratt & Whitney JT8D-11 (66.7 kN) thrust each.
