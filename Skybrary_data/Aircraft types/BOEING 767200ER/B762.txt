Medium to long range wide-body airliner. In service since 1982 (200ER since 1984). Tandem development with 757. Exists in an ER (extended range) version for Extended Range Twin Engine Operation . The B762 is member of the B767 family of aircraft. Wing span (metric): 47.60 m. Length (metric): 48.50 m. Height (metric): 15.90 m. Powerplant: 2 x P&W PW4062 (281.6 kN) or 2 x GE CF6-80C2B7F (276.2 kN).. Engine Model(s): General Electric CF6. 2 x P&W PW4062 (281.6 kN) or 2 x GE CF6-80C2B7F (276.2 kN).
