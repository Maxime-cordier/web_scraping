Stretched development of the BAe-146-100. First flight in August 1982. Total of 116 aircraft were built. Later replaced by Avro RJ 85. The B462 is member of the BAe 146 family of aircraft. Wing span (metric): 26.34 m. Length (metric): 28.60 m. Height (metric): 8.59 m. Powerplant: 4 x Honeywell ALF 502R-5 (31kN) turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Honeywell ALF502. 4 x Honeywell ALF 502R-5 (31kN) turbofans.
