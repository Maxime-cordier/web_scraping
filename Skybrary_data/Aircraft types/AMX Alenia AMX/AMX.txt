Light attack aircraft / trainer. In service since 1988. Replacement of G91 and F-104 for Italian Air Force and MB-326 Xavante for Brazilia Air Force. Two-seat trainer version AMX-T. Wing span (metric): 8.90 m. Length (metric): 13.23 m. Height (metric): 4.55 m. Powerplant: 1 x 49 kN R-R Spey Mk 807 non afterburning turbofan, built by Fiat.. Engine Model(s): Rolls-Royce Spey. 1 x 49 kN R-R Spey Mk 807 non afterburning turbofan, built by Fiat.
