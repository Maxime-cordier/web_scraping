The Ilyushin Il-38 (NATO reporting name: May) is a maritime patrol and anti-submarine warfare aircraft designed in the Soviet Union. It was a development of the Ilyushin Il-18 turboprop transport. Wing span (metric): 37.42 m. Length (metric): 39.60 m. Height (metric): 10.16 m. Powerplant: 4 x Ivchenko-Progress AI-20M turboprops, 3,170 kW (4,250 hp) each.. Engine Model(s): Ivchenko AI-20. 4 x Ivchenko-Progress AI-20M turboprops, 3,170 kW (4,250 hp) each.
