Executive transport aircraft. In service since 1977. Turbo-prop powered equivalent to CESSNA 404 Titan with bonded wet wings and pressurized fuselage. Cessna renamed the Conquest to Conquest 2 in 1986. Hybrid version F406 Caravan 2, with unpressurized fuselage of 404 Titan and wings of 441 Conquest 2, built by REIMS, France. Wing span (metric): 15.00 m. Length (metric): 11.90 m. Height (metric): 4.00 m. Powerplant: 2 x 635 SHP Garrett TPE331-8-401S/402S turboprops with 3 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): AlliedSignal TPE-331. 2 x 635 SHP Garrett TPE331-8-401S/402S turboprops with 3 blade propellers.
