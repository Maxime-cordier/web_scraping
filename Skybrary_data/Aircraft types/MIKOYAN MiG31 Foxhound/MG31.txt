Interceptor aircraft. In service since 1979. Two seat interceptor development based on MiG-25. 280 aircraft were built for Russian Air Force. Improved development MiG-31M since 1984 (only 6 prototypes and one demonstrator were built). Note: Two seat interceptor development based on MiG-25 (NATO name:Foxhound). Wing span (metric): 13.50 m. Length (metric): 22.70 m. Height (metric): 6.20 m. Powerplant: 2 x 151,9 KN Soloviev D-30F6 turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Soloviev D-30. 2 x 151,9 KN Soloviev D-30F6 turbofans.
