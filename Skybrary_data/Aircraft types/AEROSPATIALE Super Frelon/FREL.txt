The Aérospatiale SA 321 Super Frelon is a three-engined heavy transport helicopter produced by Aérospatiale of France. The helicopter is still in use in China where the locally produced version is known as the Z-8. Frelon is French for hornet. Wing span (metric): 18.90 m. Length (metric): 23.03 m. Height (metric): 6.66 m. Powerplant: 3 x Turboméca Turmo IIIC turboshafts, 1,171 kW each. Engine Model(s): Turboméca Turmo. 3 x Turboméca Turmo IIIC turboshafts, 1,171 kW each
