Narrow-body, twin-engine, medium-range jet airliners originally designed by Canadian manufacturer Bombardier Aerospace able to carry between 130 and 160 passengers. First delivery in 2016. The Bombardier CSeries aircraft contains a high proportion of composite materials and features larger windows. Wing span (metric): 35.10 m. Length (metric): 38.70 m. Height (metric): 11.50 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney PurePower PW1500G Engines (93.4 kN). Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney PW1000G. 2 x Pratt & Whitney PurePower PW1500G Engines (93.4 kN)
