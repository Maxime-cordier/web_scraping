Mid-size corporate jet. In service since 2000. IAIs new mid-size corporate jet development based on the IAI 1125 ASTRA SP, with wide-body fuselage and Electronic Flight Instrument System cockpit. Coproduction with YAKOVLEV marked by IAIs subsidiary Galaxy Aerospace. Wing span (metric): 17.40 m. Length (metric): 19.00 m. Height (metric): 6.40 m. Powerplant: 2 x 25,4 kN P&W PW306A-5 turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PW300. 2 x 25,4 kN P&W PW306A-5 turbofans.
