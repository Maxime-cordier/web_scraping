Carrier borne strike fighter. In service since since 1978. Original Etendard IV light attack fighter development for french Navy. Later developed into the more advanced Super Etendard. It has recently been modernized with latest type of avionics. Also used by Argentinian Navy. Wing span (metric): 9.60 m. Length (metric): 14.30 m. Height (metric): 3.90 m. Powerplant: 1 x 49 KN SNECMA Atar 8K-50 turbojet.. Engine Model(s): SNECMA Atar 8. 1 x 49 KN SNECMA Atar 8K-50 turbojet.
