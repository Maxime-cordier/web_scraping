The AgustaWestland AW189 is a twin-engined, medium-lift helicopter developed by AgustaWestland, and is derived from the AW149. Wing span (metric): 14.60 m. Length (metric): 17.60 m. Height (metric): 5.06 m. Powerplant: 2 x General Electric CT7-2E1 turboshaft engine, 1,492 kW each.. Engine Model(s): General Electric CT7. 2 x General Electric CT7-2E1 turboshaft engine, 1,492 kW each.
