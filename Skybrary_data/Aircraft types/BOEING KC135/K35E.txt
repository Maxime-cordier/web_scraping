Air-to-air refueling tanker. In service since 1982. Improved development of KC-135 A/B with more powerful ex. civilian TF33 engines (ICAO Designator: K35A). USAF continues to re-engine KC-135 As to KC-135 D/Es and KC-135Rs. Max Mach 0.82 Wing span (metric): 39.90 m. Length (metric): 41.50 m. Height (metric): 12.70 m. Powerplant: 4 x 93.4 kN P&W TF33-PW-100 turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney TF33. 4 x 93.4 kN P&W TF33-PW-100 turbofans.
