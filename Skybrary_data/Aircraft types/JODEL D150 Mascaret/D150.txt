The Jodel D.150 Mascaret is a French two-seat single-engined light aircraft of the 1960s built by Société Aeronautique Normande (SAN) as a replacement for the earlier Jodel D11 trainer/tourer aircraft. Wing span (metric): 8.15 m. Length (metric): 6.30 m. Height (metric): 1.76 m. Powerplant: 1 x Rolls-Royce Continental O-200-A air-cooled flat-four, 75 kW (100 hp).. Engine Model(s): Continental O-200. 1 x Rolls-Royce Continental O-200-A air-cooled flat-four, 75 kW (100 hp).
