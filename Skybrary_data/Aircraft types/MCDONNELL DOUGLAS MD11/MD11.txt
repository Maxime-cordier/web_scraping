Long range, wide-body airliner. In service since 1990. Modernised, slightly stretched and re-engined development of DC-10 Tri-jet, with winglets and advanced EFIS flightdeck. Some models were built: MD-11ER for extended range,. MD-11F freighter and. MD-11 combi freighter/airliner.. Production taken over by BOEING in 1998. Last delivery in 2001.
