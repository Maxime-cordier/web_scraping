Four jet engined corporate jet. In service since 1977 (L-1329 Jetstar since 1961). Re-engined turbofan development of turbojet powered L-1329 Jetstar (ICAO Designator: L29A). Production ceased in 1979. Wing span (metric): 16.60 m. Length (metric): 18.40 m. Height (metric): 6.20 m. Powerplant: 4 x 16,78 kN AirResearch TFE 731-3 turbofans. L-1329: 4 x 14,7 kN P&W JT12A-6 turbojets.. Engine Model(s): Garrett TFE731. 4 x 16,78 kN AirResearch TFE 731-3 turbofans. L-1329: 4 x 14,7 kN P&W JT12A-6 turbojets.
