High performance light aircraft. In service since 1977 (T182RG since 1979). Retractable gear version of CESSNA 182 with a turbocharged engine. Turbocharged fixed gear model also built in small numbers. Production ceased in 1985. Note: Has 2 side windows and flat rear window. Wing span (metric): 10.90 m. Length (metric): 8.50 m. Height (metric): 2.80 m. Powerplant: 1 x 235 HP Lycoming O-540-L3C5D turbocharged piston engine with 2 blade propeller.. Engine Model(s): Lycoming O-540. 1 x 235 HP Lycoming O-540-L3C5D turbocharged piston engine with 2 blade propeller.
