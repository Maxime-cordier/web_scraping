Medium to long range airliner/freighter. In service since 1958. First member of BOEINGs 707 production serie. First models with turbojets, later re-engined with JT3D turbofans. C-137/CC-137 military freighter version. The B701 is member of the B707 family of aircraft. Wing span (metric): 39.90 m. Length (metric): 44.07 m. Height (metric): 12.90 m. Powerplant: 4 x P&W JT3D-1 (76.2 kN) turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney JT3D. 4 x P&W JT3D-1 (76.2 kN) turbofans.
