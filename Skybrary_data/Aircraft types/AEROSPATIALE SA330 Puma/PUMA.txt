The Aérospatiale Puma is a four-bladed, twin-engined medium transport/utility helicopter. The Puma was originally manufactured by Sud Aviation of France under the designation SA.330. Wing span (metric): 15.00 m. Length (metric): 18.15 m. Height (metric): 5.14 m. Powerplant: 2 x Turboméca Turmo IVC turboshafts, 1,175 kW (1,575 hp) each. 2 x Turboméca Turmo IVC turboshafts, 1,175 kW (1,575 hp) each
