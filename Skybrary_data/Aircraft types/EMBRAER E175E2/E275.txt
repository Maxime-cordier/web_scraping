Medium-range jet airliner developed by Embraer, a member of the E-Jet E2 family which succeeded the original E-Jet. Reduced fuel consumption and increased range and capacity compared to previous models E75S and E75L. Wing span (metric): 31.00 m. Length (metric): 32.40 m. Height (metric): 9.98 m. Powerplant: 2 x 67kN Pratt & Whitney PW1700G turbofans. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney PW1000G. 2 x 67kN Pratt & Whitney PW1700G turbofans
