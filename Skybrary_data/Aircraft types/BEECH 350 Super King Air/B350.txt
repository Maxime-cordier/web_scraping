Corporate and utility aircraft. In service since 1989. Stretched and improved replacement of the Beech 300 Super King Air (which only saw limited production). Production switched 1991 to RAYTHEON, USA. Wing span (metric): 17.70 m. Length (metric): 14.20 m. Height (metric): 4.40 m. Powerplant: 2 x P&W PT6A-60A (1.050 SHP) turboprop with 4 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6. 2 x P&W PT6A-60A (1.050 SHP) turboprop with 4 blade propellers.
