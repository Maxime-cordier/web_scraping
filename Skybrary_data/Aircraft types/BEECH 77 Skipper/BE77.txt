The Model 77 Skipper is a two-seat, fixed tricycle gear general aviation airplane, originally designed for flight training, and later used as well for touring and personal flying. Wing span (metric): 9.10 m. Length (metric): 7.30 m. Height (metric): 1.81 m. Powerplant: 1 x Lycoming O-235-L2C flat-4 engine, 86 kW.. Engine Model(s): Lycoming O-235. 1 x Lycoming O-235-L2C flat-4 engine, 86 kW.
