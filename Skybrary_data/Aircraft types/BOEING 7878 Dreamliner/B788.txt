The Boeing 787-8 Dreamliner is a long range, mid-sized, wide-body, twin-engine jet airliner. The B788 is a member of the B787 series of aircraft.The 787 is the world's first airliner using composite materials for most of its construction (approx. 50 percent). Wing span (metric): 60.10 m. Length (metric): 56.70 m. Height (metric): 16.90 m. Powerplant: 2 x GEnx-1B (280 kN) or 2 x R-R Trent 1000 (280 kN) turbofans.. Engine Model(s): General Electric GEnx. 2 x GEnx-1B (280 kN) or 2 x R-R Trent 1000 (280 kN) turbofans.
