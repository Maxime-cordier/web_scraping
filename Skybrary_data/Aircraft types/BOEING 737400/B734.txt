Short to medium range airliner. In service since 1988. Similar stretched version of 737-300 introduced as replacement for the 727. For more information, see Boeing's B737 family specifications. Wing span (metric): 28.90 m. Length (metric): 36.48 m. Height (metric): 11.13 m. Powerplant: 2 x CFM56-3B2 (97.9 kN) or 2 x CFM56-3C (105 kN) turbofans.. Engine Model(s): CFM International CFM56. 2 x CFM56-3B2 (97.9 kN) or 2 x CFM56-3C (105 kN) turbofans.
