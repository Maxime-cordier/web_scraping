The Agusta A129 Mangusta is an attack helicopter originally designed and produced by Agusta in Italy. Wing span (metric): 11.90 m. Length (metric): 12.16 m. Height (metric): 3.42 m. Powerplant: 2 x Rolls-Royce Gem 2-1004D (license built by Piaggio) turboshafts, 664 kW (890 shp) each.. Engine Model(s): Rolls-Royce Gem. 2 x Rolls-Royce Gem 2-1004D (license built by Piaggio) turboshafts, 664 kW (890 shp) each.
