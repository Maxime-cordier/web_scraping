Light turbofan corporate jet. In service since 1988. Improved development of Learjet 35/36, combined fuselage and powerplant of Learjet 35/36 with modern wings of Learjet 55/60 (deltafins below the T-tail). Models 31A/ER for extended range with higher take-off weight. Gates Learjet is now part of the Bombardier Canada Group. Wing span (metric): 13.50 m. Length (metric): 14.80 m. Height (metric): 3.80 m. Powerplant: 2 x 15.6 kN Garrett TFE731-2B turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Garrett TFE731. 2 x 15.6 kN Garrett TFE731-2B turbofans.
