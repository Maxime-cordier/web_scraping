The Beechcraft Premier I is a light jet aircraft made by the Beechcraft division of Hawker Beechcraft. The aircraft was designed to compete with the Cessna CitationJet series of aircraft. Wing span (metric): 13.56 m. Length (metric): 14.02 m. Height (metric): 4.67 m. Powerplant: 2 x Williams / Rolls-Royce FJ44-2A. Engine Model(s): Williams FJ44. 2 x Williams / Rolls-Royce FJ44-2A
