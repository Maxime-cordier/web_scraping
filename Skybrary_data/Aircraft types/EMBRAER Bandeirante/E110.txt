Twin turboprop commuter airliner. In service since 1973. Very popular unpressurised regional airliner. Exists in many versions: EMB-110 12 seat transport,. EMB-110B for air photography,. EMB-111 maritime patrol for Brazil Air Force,. EMB-110C 15 seat transport,. EMB-110E 7 seat executive transport,. EMB-110PA improved version with dihedral tail plane and some military versions.. Production ceased in 1990. EMB-111: Wing span 15,9 m Length 14,91 m MTOW 7,000kg. Wing span (metric): 15.32 m. Length (metric): 15.10 m. Height (metric): 4.92 m. Powerplant: 2 x 680 SHP P&W PT6A-27 or 2 x 750 SHP P&W PT6A-34 turboprops with 3 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6. 2 x 680 SHP P&W PT6A-27 or 2 x 750 SHP P&W PT6A-34 turboprops with 3 blade propellers.
