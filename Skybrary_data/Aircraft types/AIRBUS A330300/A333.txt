Large capacity long range airliner. In service since 1993. Largest member of Airbus twinjet family (2-engine version of A340). Total of 253 aircraft ordered and 193 in operation (August 2006). Wing span (metric): 60.30 m. Length (metric): 63.69 m. Height (metric): 16.83 m. Powerplant: 2 x GE CF6-80E1 (306kN) or 2 x R-R Trent 772 (300kN) or 2 x PW 4000 (308kN) turbofans.. Engine Model(s): General Electric CF6. 2 x GE CF6-80E1 (306kN) or 2 x R-R Trent 772 (300kN) or 2 x PW 4000 (308kN) turbofans.
