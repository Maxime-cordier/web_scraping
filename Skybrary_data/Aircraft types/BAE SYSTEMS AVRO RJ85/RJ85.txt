Short range regional airliner. Avroliner 85 signifies for 85 passengers. Successor of the BAe-146-200. Production was switched to RJ 85 in 1993. The RJ85 features an improved cabin and the more efficient LF 507's. Total of 87 aircraft were built. Wing span (metric): 26.34 m. Length (metric): 28.55 m. Height (metric): 8.61 m. Powerplant: 4 x 31.15 kN Textron Lycoming LF 507 turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Lycoming LF507. 4 x 31.15 kN Textron Lycoming LF 507 turbofans.
