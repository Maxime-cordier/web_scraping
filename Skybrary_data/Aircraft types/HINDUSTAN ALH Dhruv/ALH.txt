The HAL Dhruv is a utility helicopter developed and manufactured by India's Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL). Wing span (metric): 13.20 m. Length (metric): 15.87 m. Height (metric): 4.98 m. Powerplant: 2 × Shakti turboshafts (Turbomeca Ardiden), 1,000 kW. Engine Model(s): HAL/Turboméca Shakti. 2 × Shakti turboshafts (Turbomeca Ardiden), 1,000 kW
