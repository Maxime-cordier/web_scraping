Short range high capacity wide-body airliner. In service since 1983, it is a special version of the 747-100 with the stretched upper deck of the 747-300 and strengthened landing gear and was developed specifically for Japanese short haul (SR for short range) domestic sectors. The B747SR is a member of the B747 family of aircraft. Wing span (metric): 59.60 m. Length (metric): 70.60 m. Height (metric): 19.30 m. Powerplant: 4 x P&W JT9D-7A (209.9 kN) or 4 x P&W JT9D-7F (213.5 kN) turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney JT9D. 4 x P&W JT9D-7A (209.9 kN) or 4 x P&W JT9D-7F (213.5 kN) turbofans.
