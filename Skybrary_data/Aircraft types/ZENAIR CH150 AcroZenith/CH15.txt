The Zenith CH 150 Acro Zenith is a Canadian single-engine, low wing, all-aluminum aircraft designed by Chris Heintz and produced by Zenair in kit form for amateur construction. Wing span (metric): 6.15 m. Length (metric): 6.17 m. Height (metric): 1.52 m. Powerplant: 1 x Lycoming 0-320, 110 kW (150 hp).. Engine Model(s): Lycoming O-320. 1 x Lycoming 0-320, 110 kW (150 hp).
