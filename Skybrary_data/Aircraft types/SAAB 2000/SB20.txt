Turboprop regional airliner. In service since 1994. Stretched, more powerful development of SAAB 340 regional airliner. First civil aircraft powered by Allison AE-2100A turboprops (developed for the V-22 Osprey tiltrotor). Cabin noise is reduced by an active noise control system with 72 microphones and 36 speakers which generate an anti-phase noise. 56 aircraft were built until 1998 and production then ceased. Wing span (metric): 24.80 m. Length (metric): 27.30 m. Height (metric): 7.70 m. Powerplant: 2 x 4.125 SHP Allison AE-2100A turboprops with 6 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Rolls-Royce AE 2100. 2 x 4.125 SHP Allison AE-2100A turboprops with 6 blade propellers.
