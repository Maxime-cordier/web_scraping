Large capacity long range airliner. In service since 1998. Shorter long range version of A-330-300 ( 2-engine version of A340) to supplement the A-300-600. Full freighter version A330-200F (first delivery in 2004). Total of 327 aircraft ordered and 229 in operation (August 2006). Wing span (metric): 60.30 m. Length (metric): 58.82 m. Height (metric): 17.39 m. Powerplant: 2 x GE CF6-80E1 (306kN) or 2 x R-R Trent 772 (300kN) or 2 x PW 4173 (308kN) turbofans.. Engine Model(s): General Electric CF6. 2 x GE CF6-80E1 (306kN) or 2 x R-R Trent 772 (300kN) or 2 x PW 4173 (308kN) turbofans.
