Turboprop version of the PZL-MIELEC Dromader powered with Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6A-45 engine. First flew in 1985 and was given FAA certification in April 1986. Wing span (metric): 17.70 m. Length (metric): 9.50 m. Height (metric): 3.70 m. Powerplant: 1 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6A-45. 1 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6A-45
