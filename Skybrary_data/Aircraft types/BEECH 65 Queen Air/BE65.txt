Utility, light transport and commuter airliner. In service since 1960. Initial model of BEECH largest piston engine utility aircraft family. Combined the wing of the BEECH 50 Twin Bonanza with a new fuselage. Replaced by BEECH 80 in 1964. Mil. Type: U-8F Seminole. Wing span (metric): 15.50 m. Length (metric): 10.80 m. Height (metric): 4.30 m. Powerplant: 2 x 340 HP Lycoming IGSO-480-A1E6 piston engines with three blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Lycoming O-480. 2 x 340 HP Lycoming IGSO-480-A1E6 piston engines with three blade propellers.
