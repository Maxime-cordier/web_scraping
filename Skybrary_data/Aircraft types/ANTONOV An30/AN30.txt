Aerial survey and mapping aircraft. In service since the early seventies. Special version of An-24 with a glazing nose for survey and cartography is used by many countries in the open skies programme. Can easily be turned into and is often used as an An-26 type transport. Wing span (metric): 29.20 m. Length (metric): 24.30 m. Height (metric): 8.30 m. Powerplant: 2 x 2.820 SHP Ivchenko AI-24VT turboprops with 4 blade propellers..
