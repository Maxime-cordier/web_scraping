The Airbus Helicopters EC175 (also Avicopter Z-15) is a 7-ton class medium utility helicopter being developed by Airbus Helicopters (formerly Eurocopter) Group and AVIC. Wing span (metric): 14.80 m. Length (metric): 15.68 m. Height (metric): 5.34 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6C-67E turboshaft, 1,324 kW each.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6. 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6C-67E turboshaft, 1,324 kW each.
