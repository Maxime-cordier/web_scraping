The Eclipse 500 is a small six-seat business jet aircraft manufactured by Eclipse Aviation. The model 500 became the first of a new class of Very Light Jet when it was delivered in late 2006. The aircraft is powered by two lightweight Pratt & Whitney Canada PW610F turbofan engines in aft fuselage-mounted nacelles. Wing span (metric): 11.40 m. Length (metric): 10.10 m. Height (metric): 3.40 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PW610F turbofan engines, 4 kN each.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PW600. 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PW610F turbofan engines, 4 kN each.
