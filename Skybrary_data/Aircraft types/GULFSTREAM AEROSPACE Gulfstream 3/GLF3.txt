Long range corporate jet. In service since 1980. Improved development of G-1159 Gulfstream 2 with a revised wing, larger wing span, stretched fuselage and winglets. Production changed to G-1159C Gulfstream 4 in 1986. Mil. types: C-20A to E. Wing span (metric): 23.70 m. Length (metric): 25.30 m. Height (metric): 7.40 m. Powerplant: 2 x 50.7 kN R-R Spey Mk 511-8 turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Rolls-Royce Spey. 2 x 50.7 kN R-R Spey Mk 511-8 turbofans.
