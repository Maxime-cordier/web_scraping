The Bell AH-1 SuperCobra is a twin-engine attack helicopter based on the US Army's AH-1 Cobra. The twin Cobra family includes the AH-1J SeaCobra, the AH-1T Improved SeaCobra, and the AH-1W SuperCobra. Wing span (metric): 14.60 m. Length (metric): 13.60 m. Height (metric): 4.10 m. Powerplant: 2 x General Electric T700 turboshaft, 1,680 shp (1,300 kW) each. Engine Model(s): General Electric CT7. 2 x General Electric T700 turboshaft, 1,680 shp (1,300 kW) each
