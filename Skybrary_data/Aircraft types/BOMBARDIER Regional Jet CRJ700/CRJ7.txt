Regional airliner. In service since 2001. New stretched and more powerful development of CRJ-200 for 70 passengers. Canadair is now part of the Bombardier Group. The CRJ7 is a member of the CRJ family. Wing span (metric): 23.24 m. Length (metric): 32.51 m. Height (metric): 7.57 m. Powerplant: 2 x 56.4 or 61.3 kN rated GE CF34 - 8C1 turbofans.. Engine Model(s): General Electric CF34. 2 x 56.4 or 61.3 kN rated GE CF34 - 8C1 turbofans.
