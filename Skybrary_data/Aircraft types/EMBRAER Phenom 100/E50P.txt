The Embraer Phenom 100 is a very light jet developed by Brazilian aircraft manufacturer Embraer. Wing span (metric): 12.30 m. Length (metric): 12.82 m. Height (metric): 4.35 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PW617F-E turbofans, 7.2 kN each.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PW600. 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PW617F-E turbofans, 7.2 kN each.
