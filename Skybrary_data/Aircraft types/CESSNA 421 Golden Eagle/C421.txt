Utility twin engine aircraft. In service since 1967. Pressurized development of CESSNA 411. Improved version CESSNA 421B Golden Eagle with lengthened nose and wing span. Last version (from 1975) CESSNA 421C with bonded wet wing, no tip tanks and higher vertical tail. Production ceased in 1985. Wing span (metric): 12.20 m. Length (metric): 10.30 m. Height (metric): 3.60 m. Powerplant: 2 x 375 HP Continental GTSIO-520-H turbocharged piston engine with 3 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Continental O-520. 2 x 375 HP Continental GTSIO-520-H turbocharged piston engine with 3 blade propellers.
