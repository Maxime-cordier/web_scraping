The Bell 214ST is a medium-lift, twin-engine helicopter descended from Bell Helicopter's ubiquitous UH-1 Huey series. Though it shares a type number with the somewhat-related Bell 214, the 214ST is larger and of quite different appearance. Wing span (metric): 15.85 m. Length (metric): 15.03 m. Height (metric): 4.84 m. Powerplant: 2 x General Electric CT7-2A turboshaft, 1,625 shp (1,215 kW) each. Engine Model(s): General Electric CT7. 2 x General Electric CT7-2A turboshaft, 1,625 shp (1,215 kW) each
