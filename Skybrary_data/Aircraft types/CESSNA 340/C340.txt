Business aircraft. In service since 1971. Similar production to BEECHs Duke, slotting in between CESSNA 310 and CESSNA 414/421 with 414s wings and 310s undercarriage and tail unit. Introduction of a pressuration system. Production ended in 1984. CESSNA 335 unpressurized, light weight and lower cost development. Built from 1979 to 1980. Wing span (metric): 11.60 m. Length (metric): 10.50 m. Height (metric): 3.80 m. Powerplant: 2 x 285 HP Continental TSIO-520-K or. Engine Model(s): Continental O-520. 2 x 285 HP Continental TSIO-520-K or
