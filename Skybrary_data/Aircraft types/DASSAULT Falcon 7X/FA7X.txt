The Dassault Falcon 7X is a three-engine long-range corporate jet aircraft produced by the French manufacturer Dassault Aviation. Wing span (metric): 26.21 m. Length (metric): 23.19 m. Height (metric): 7.80 m. Powerplant: 3 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PW307A, 28,5 kN each.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PW300. 3 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PW307A, 28,5 kN each.
