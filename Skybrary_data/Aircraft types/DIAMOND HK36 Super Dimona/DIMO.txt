The Diamond HK36 Super Dimona is an extensive family of Austrian low-wing, T-tailed, two-seat motor gliders that were designed by Wolf Hoffmann and currently produced by Diamond Aircraft Industries. Wing span (metric): 16.50 m. Length (metric): 7.10 m. Height (metric): 1.70 m. Powerplant: 1 x Rotax 912, 80 hp (60 kW).. Engine Model(s): Rotax 912. 1 x Rotax 912, 80 hp (60 kW).
