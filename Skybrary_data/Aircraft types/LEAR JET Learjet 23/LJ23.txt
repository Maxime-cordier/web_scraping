The Learjet 23 is an American six to eight seat (two crew and four to six passengers) twin-engined, high speed business jet. Manufactured by Learjet, the LJ23 was revolutionary in the aviation world. Wing span (metric): 10.84 m. Length (metric): 13.18 m. Height (metric): 3.84 m. Powerplant: 2 x General Electric CJ610-4 turbojets, 2,850 lbf (12.71 kN) each.. Engine Model(s): General Electric CJ610. 2 x General Electric CJ610-4 turbojets, 2,850 lbf (12.71 kN) each.
