The AgustaWestland AW169 is a twin-engine, 10-seat helicopter currently developed by AgustaWestland. Wing span (metric): 12.12 m. Length (metric): 14.65 m. Height (metric): 4.50 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PW210 turboshaft engine, 750 kW each.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PW200. 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PW210 turboshaft engine, 750 kW each.
