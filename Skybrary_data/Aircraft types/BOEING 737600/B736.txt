Short range airliner. In service since 1998. The first and shortest version of the BOEING 737 third generation called next generation. All versions have more powerful and efficient engines, improved wings and tail sections and modernized cockpits. Replaced the BOEING 737-500. For more information, see Boeing's B737 family specifications. Wing span (metric): 34.30 m. Length (metric): 31.27 m. Height (metric): 12.60 m. Powerplant: 2 x CFM56-7B (97,9 kN) turbofans.. Engine Model(s): CFM International CFM56. 2 x CFM56-7B (97,9 kN) turbofans.
