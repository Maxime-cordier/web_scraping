Executive corporate jet. In service since 1979. Improved, stretched turbofan powered development of NA-265-60. Production ceased in 1981. Most are still in service. Both models are used by several military organisations and American Federal services. Wing span (metric): 13.60 m. Length (metric): 14.40 m. Height (metric): 5.30 m. Powerplant: 2 x 19.57 kN GE CF700-2D-2 turbofans.. Engine Model(s): General Electric CF700. 2 x 19.57 kN GE CF700-2D-2 turbofans.
