The Lancair IV and IV-P are a family of four-seat low-wing retractable-gear composite monoplanes powered by a 550 cubic inch Continental turbo-normalised piston engine. Lancair IV-P is the pressurized version. Wing span (metric): 10.82 m. Length (metric): 7.62 m. Height (metric): 2.40 m. Powerplant: 1 x Continental TSIO-550 six cylinder, horizontally opposed piston engine, 350 hp.. Engine Model(s): Continental IO-550. 1 x Continental TSIO-550 six cylinder, horizontally opposed piston engine, 350 hp.
