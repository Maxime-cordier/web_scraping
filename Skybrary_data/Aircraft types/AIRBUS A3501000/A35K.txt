The A350-1000 is the largest variant of the A350 family and is to seat 350 passengers in a three-class cabin and 9-abreast configuration. It will have a range of 8,400 nm. It is designed to replace the Airbus A340-600. Wing span (metric): 64.75 m. Length (metric): 73.78 m. Height (metric): 17.08 m. Powerplant: 2 x R-R Trent XWB (431 kN).. Engine Model(s): Rolls-Royce Trent XWB. 2 x R-R Trent XWB (431 kN).
