Corporate and utility aircraft. In service since 1981. Turbocharged model of CESSNA 303 to replace the CESSNA 310. Production ceased in 1985 after 297 aircraft were built. Wing span (metric): 11.90 m. Length (metric): 9.30 m. Height (metric): 4.10 m. Powerplant: 2 x 250 HP Continental TSIO 520 - AE turbocharged piston engines with 3 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Continental O-520. 2 x 250 HP Continental TSIO 520 - AE turbocharged piston engines with 3 blade propellers.
