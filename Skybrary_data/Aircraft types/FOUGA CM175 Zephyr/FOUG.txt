The Fouga Zéphyr (company designation CM.175) was a 1950s French two-seat carrier-capable jet trainer for the French Navy. It was developed from the land-based CM.170 Magister. Wing span (metric): 12.15 m. Length (metric): 10.06 m. Height (metric): 2.80 m. Powerplant: 2 x Turbomeca Marboré IIA turbojets, 3.92 kN each. Engine Model(s): Turboméca Marboré. 2 x Turbomeca Marboré IIA turbojets, 3.92 kN each
