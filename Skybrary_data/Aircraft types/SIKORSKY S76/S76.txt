The Sikorsky S-76 is an American multi-purpose medium-size commercial helicopter. The S-76 is powered by two turboshafts which combine to drive the main and tail rotors, each with four blades. The S-76 landing gear is retractable. Wing span (metric): 13.41 m. Length (metric): 16.00 m. Height (metric): 4.41 m. Powerplant: 2 x Turboméca Arriel 2S2 turboshafts, over 500 kW each.. Engine Model(s): Turboméca Arriel. 2 x Turboméca Arriel 2S2 turboshafts, over 500 kW each.
