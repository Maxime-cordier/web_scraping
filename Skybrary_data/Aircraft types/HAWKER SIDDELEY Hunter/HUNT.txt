Transonic jet fighter/fighter-bomber aircraft developed in the 1950's. On 7 September 1953, the first prototype broke the world air speed record for jet-powered aircraft. Wing span (metric): 10.26 m. Length (metric): 14.00 m. Height (metric): 4.01 m. Powerplant: 1 x Rolls-Royce Avon 207 turbojet (45.13 kN). Engine Model(s): Rolls-Royce Avon. 1 x Rolls-Royce Avon 207 turbojet (45.13 kN)
