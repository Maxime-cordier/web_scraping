The Scheibe SF-25 Falke (English: Falcon) is a two-seater German touring motor glider manufactured by Scheibe Aircraft GmbH. Wing span (metric): 15.30 m. Length (metric): 7.60 m. Height (metric): 1.68 m. Powerplant: 1 x Rotax 912 A flat-4, air-cooled with water-cooled cylinder heads, 65 shp (48 kW ). Engine Model(s): Rotax 912. 1 x Rotax 912 A flat-4, air-cooled with water-cooled cylinder heads, 65 shp (48 kW )
