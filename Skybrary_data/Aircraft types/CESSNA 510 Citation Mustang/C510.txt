The Cessna Citation Mustang, Model 510, is a very light jet (VLJ) class business jet built by Cessna Aircraft Company at their Independence, Kansas production facility. The Mustang, in standard configuration, has four passenger seats in the aft cabin, a toilet and seating for two in the cockpit. Like most other light jets, the Mustang is approved for single-pilot operation. Wing span (metric): 13.16 m. Length (metric): 12.37 m. Height (metric): 4.09 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PW615F turbofan, 6.49 kN each. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PW600. 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PW615F turbofan, 6.49 kN each
