The E190 first flew in March 2004. On the 8th of November 2005 it entered service with launch customer Jetblue Airways. By January 2010, 263 aircraft have been delivered with another 185 on order. The E190 is a member of the E Jets Family of aircraft. Wing span (metric): 28.72 m. Length (metric): 36.24 m. Height (metric): 10.57 m. Powerplant: 2 General Electric (CF34-10E) 82.3kN (18,500lb) turbofans.. Engine Model(s): General Electric CF34. 2 General Electric (CF34-10E) 82.3kN (18,500lb) turbofans.
