The Boeing 767 is a mid-size, wide-body twinjet airliner produced by Boeing Commercial Airplanes. Passenger versions of the 767 can carry between 181 and 375 passengers, and have a range of 5200 nm to 6590 nm depending on variant and seating configuration. The Boeing 767 has been produced in three fuselage lengths: the original 767-200 first entered into airline service in 1982,. followed by the 767-300 in 1986, and the. 767-400ER in 2000.. Extended range versions of the original -200 and -300 models, the 767-200ER and 767-300ER, have been produced with added payload and operating distance capability.
