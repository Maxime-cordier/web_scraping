An improved hot/high perrformance version of the ATR 72-200 with PW127 engines rated at 1,849kW (2,480shp). ATCPS power 2,059 kW (2,760shp). Carries 17 to 19 more passengers than standard ATR 72. View the [http://www.skybrary.aero/bookshelf/books/3697.pdf manufacturer's factsheet] which also includes data on the ATR 72-200 (ICAO code AT72). Wing span (metric): 27.05 m. Length (metric): 27.17 m. Height (metric): 7.65 m. Powerplant: 2 x 2.480 SHP PWC PW127E turboprops with 6 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PW100. 2 x 2.480 SHP PWC PW127E turboprops with 6 blade propellers.
