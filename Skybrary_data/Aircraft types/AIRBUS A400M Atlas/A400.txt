The Airbus A400M Atlas is a multi-national four-engine turboprop military transport aircraft. It was designed by Airbus Military as a tactical airlifter with strategic capabilities. The aircraft's maiden flight took place on 11 December 2009. Wing span (metric): 45.10 m. Length (metric): 42.40 m. Height (metric): 14.70 m. Powerplant: 4 × Europrop TP400-D6 turboprop (8,250 kW).. Engine Model(s): Europrop International TP400. 4 × Europrop TP400-D6 turboprop (8,250 kW).
