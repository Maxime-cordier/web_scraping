Regional airliner. In service since 1991 (L-610G/AYRES 700 since 1999). Stretched development of L-410 as 40 seat commuter airliner for AEROFLOT (L-610M) only a small number delivered. Improved west market version L-610G with CT7 turbofans and Electronic Flight Instrument System avionics. Provisional customer is Czech airline CSA. Development continued, since 2000 as AYRES 700.
