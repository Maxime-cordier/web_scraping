Medium-range jet airliner developed by Embraer, a member of the E-Jet E2 family which succeeded the original E-Jet. Reduced fuel consumption and increased range and capacity compared to previous model E190. First flight in 2016, received type certificate in 2018. Wing span (metric): 33.70 m. Length (metric): 36.20 m. Height (metric): 11.00 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney PW1900G turbofans (85-102kN). Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney PW1000G. 2 x Pratt & Whitney PW1900G turbofans (85-102kN)
