Commuter airliner. In service since 1968. Commuter development based on BEECH Queen Air fuselage with BEECH King Air power plant to replace the BEECH 18. Some sub-models with different engines and weight were built between 1967 and 1986. Wing span (metric): 14.00 m. Length (metric): 13.60 m. Height (metric): 4.40 m. Powerplant: 2 x 680 SHP PWC PT6A-28 or. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6. 2 x 680 SHP PWC PT6A-28 or
