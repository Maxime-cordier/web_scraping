Twin turboprop regional airliner. First delivery in March 2002. Manufactured in Ukraine (Kharkov), Russian Federation (Samara) and Iran (under license, by HESA). A military version exists with patrol, surveillance, photographic and other variants for military operations. Wing span (metric): 25.51 m. Length (metric): 26.61 m. Height (metric): 8.23 m. Powerplant: 2 x 1839 kW (2466 shp) AI-30 Series 1 turbofans. 2 x 1839 kW (2466 shp) AI-30 Series 1 turbofans
