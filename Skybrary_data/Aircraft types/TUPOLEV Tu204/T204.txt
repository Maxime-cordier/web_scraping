Medium range airliner. In service since 1996. Developed in a few versions: TU-204C freighter version, TU-214 combi version, TU-204-120 and TU-204-220 Rolls Royce engine powered versions with Collins or Russian avionics. TU-224 Rolls Royce powered and TU-234 PS90 powered shorter versions for 166 passengers. The freighter version, Tu-204-120CE, powered by RR 211 engines, with English-language flight deck and Operations documentation was the first Russian/CIS built aircraft to hold an EASA aircraft type certification. Wing span (metric): 42.00 m. Length (metric): 46.00 m. Height (metric): 13.90 m. Powerplant: 204-200: 2 x 157 KN Soloviev PS-90A turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Rolls-Royce RB211. 204-200: 2 x 157 KN Soloviev PS-90A turbofans.
