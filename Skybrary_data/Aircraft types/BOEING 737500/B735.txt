Short range airliner. In service since 1990. Short fuselage version of 737-300 with better field and climb performance. For more information, see Boeing's B737 family specifications. Wing span (metric): 28.80 m. Length (metric): 31.06 m. Height (metric): 11.13 m. Powerplant: 2 x CFM56-3B4 (82 kN) or 2 x CFM56-3B1 (89 kN) turbofans, optional derated to 82.3 kN.. Engine Model(s): CFM International CFM56. 2 x CFM56-3B4 (82 kN) or 2 x CFM56-3B1 (89 kN) turbofans, optional derated to 82.3 kN.
