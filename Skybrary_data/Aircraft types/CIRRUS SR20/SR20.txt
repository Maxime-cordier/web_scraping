The Cirrus SR20 is an American piston engine, five-seat, composite monoplane built by Cirrus Aircraft of Duluth, Minnesota. Wing span (metric): 11.68 m. Length (metric): 7.92 m. Height (metric): 2.71 m. Powerplant: 1 x Continental IO-360-ES, 200 hp (149 kW).. Engine Model(s): Continental IV-1430. 1 x Continental IO-360-ES, 200 hp (149 kW).
