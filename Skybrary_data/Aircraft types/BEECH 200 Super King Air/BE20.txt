Corporate, passenger and utility aircraft. In service since 1974. Exists in many versions. 200T/ 200CT with tip tanks, 200ES with Electronic Flight Instrument System features and 200S/B200S for navaid calibration and maritime patrol. This type has been taken over and is partly built by RAYTHEON, USA. US-Mil. type: C-12. Wing span (metric): 16.60 m. Length (metric): 13.40 m. Height (metric): 4.50 m. Powerplant: 2 x 850 SHP P&W PT6A-42 turboprops with 3 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6. 2 x 850 SHP P&W PT6A-42 turboprops with 3 blade propellers.
