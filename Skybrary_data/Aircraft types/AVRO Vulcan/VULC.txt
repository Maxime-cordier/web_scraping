The Avro Vulcan (sometimes referred to as the Hawker Siddeley Vulcan) was a jet-powered delta wing strategic bomber, which was operated by the Royal Air Force (RAF) from 1956 until 1984. Aircraft manufacturer A.V. Roe and Company (Avro) designed the Vulcan. Note: The ICAO Designator VULC has been removed from ICAO Doc 8643 as aircraft of this type no longer exist in airworthy condition. Wing span (metric): 30.03 m. Length (metric): 29.59 m.
