The DG Flugzeugbau DG-1000T is a two-seater glider built by DG Flugzeugbau with 18 or 20m wingspan. Wing span (metric): 20.00 m. Length (metric): 8.57 m. Height (metric): 1.00 m. Powerplant: 1 x SOLO 2350C 2-cylinder, 2-stroke engine, 30 shp (22 kW). 1 x SOLO 2350C 2-cylinder, 2-stroke engine, 30 shp (22 kW)
