Medium-range jet airliner developed by Embraer, a member of the E-Jet E2 family which succeeded the original E-Jet. Reduced fuel consumption and increased range and capacity compared to previous model E195. First flight in 2017. Wing span (metric): 35.10 m. Length (metric): 41.50 m. Height (metric): 10.90 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney PW1900G turbofans (85-102kN). Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney PW1000G. 2 x Pratt & Whitney PW1900G turbofans (85-102kN)
