The A350-800 is to seat 270 passengers in a three-class configuration with a 9-abreast seating, and have a range of 8,300 nm. It is designed to compete to directly replace the Airbus A330-200. Note: The production of this aircraft was cancelled and consequently, the ICAO Designator A358 was from ICAO Doc 8643. Wing span (metric): 64.75 m. Length (metric): 60.54 m. Height (metric): 17.05 m. Powerplant: 2 x R-R Trent XWB (337 kN).
