The BAC 1-11 is classic British built jet airliner. Since 1964, total of 235 aircraft were built in the UK with a further 9 completed in Romania. Production stopped in 1984. There are 25 aircraft remaining in service (August 2006). Wing span (metric): 28.50 m. Length (metric): 32.61 m. Height (metric): 7.47 m. Powerplant: 2 x 55.8kN R-R Spey 512-14 DW.
