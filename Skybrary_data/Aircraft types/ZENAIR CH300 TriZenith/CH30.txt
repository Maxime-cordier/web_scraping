The Zenair Tri-Z CH 300 is a three-seat Canadian homebuilt light aircraft. A single-engined low-winged monoplane. Wing span (metric): 8.10 m. Length (metric): 6.85 m. Height (metric): 2.08 m. Powerplant: 1 x Lycoming O-320 air-cooled flat-four, 150 hp (110 kW).. Engine Model(s): Lycoming O-320. 1 x Lycoming O-320 air-cooled flat-four, 150 hp (110 kW).
