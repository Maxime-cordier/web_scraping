Executive transport aircraft. In service since 1980. Larger T-tail development of PA-31T Cheyenne 2. Replaced by the more powerful PA-42-720. Licence production by AICSA, Columbia. Airliner version PA-31T3, T-400 Cheyenne 400. Wing span (metric): 14.50 m. Length (metric): 13.20 m. Height (metric): 4.50 m. Powerplant: 2 x 720 SHP P&W PT6A-41 turboprops with 3 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6. 2 x 720 SHP P&W PT6A-41 turboprops with 3 blade propellers.
