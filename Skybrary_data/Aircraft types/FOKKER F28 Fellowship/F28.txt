Short range regional airliner. In service since 1969. Developed to complete the F-27 Friendship turboprop airliner with a higher performance and larger capacity. A family of aircraft with versions F-28-1000/2000/3000/4000/6000 with different performance regarding approach category and weight data. Also used in military service as VIP and government transport.
