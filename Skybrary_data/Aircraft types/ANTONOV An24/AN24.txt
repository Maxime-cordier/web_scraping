Regional airliner and freighter. In service since 1963. Original aircraft of the Antonov family of twin turboprop civil and military transports. Developed to AN-26, An30, AN32. Chinese version Y-7 series. More than 1100 An-24s were built. Approximately 880 remain in commercial service. Ukrainian production ceased in 1978, Chinese production continues.
