Light aircraft. In service since 1977. Retractable gear development of PA-32 Cherokee six. Replaced by Lance series in 1979 and later by Saratoga SP. Licence production by CHINCUL, Argentina, AICSA, Columbia, and by EMBRAER/NAIVA, Brazil, as EMB-721 Sertanejo. Wing span (metric): 11.00 m. Length (metric): 8.60 m. Height (metric): 2.60 m. Powerplant: 1 x 300 HP Lycoming IO-540-K1G5 piston engine with 2 or 3 blade propeller.. Engine Model(s): Lycoming O-540. 1 x 300 HP Lycoming IO-540-K1G5 piston engine with 2 or 3 blade propeller.
