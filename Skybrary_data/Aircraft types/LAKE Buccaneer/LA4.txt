The Lake Buccaneer is an American four-seat light amphibious aircraft originally developed as the Colonial C-2 Skimmer, itself a development of the two-seat Colonial C-1 Skimmer. A cantilever shoulder-wing monoplane with a single-step all metal hull with retractable tricycle landing gear. Powered by an Avco Lycoming piston engine in pusher configuration and pylon mounted above the hull. Wing span (metric): 11.58 m. Length (metric): 7.59 m. Height (metric): 2.84 m. Powerplant: 1 x Avco Lycoming IO-360-B1A piston engine, 200 hp (149 kW).. 1 x Avco Lycoming IO-360-B1A piston engine, 200 hp (149 kW).
