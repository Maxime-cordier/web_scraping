Short to medium range airliner. In service since 1980. MD-80 series: 81, 82 and 88 are very similar in terms of performance. ICAO designators: MCDONNELL DOUGLAS MD-81, MD82 and MCDONNELL DOUGLAS MD-88. Produced until December 1999. Wing span (metric): 32.78 m. Length (metric): 45.06 m. Height (metric): 9.02 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney JT8D-217A/C (88,96 kN).. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney JT8D. 2 x Pratt & Whitney JT8D-217A/C (88,96 kN).
