Light aircraft. In service since 1976. Retractable gear development of PA-28-180 Cherokee. Replaced by the stretched more powerful PA-28R-200 Arrow 2 and in 1975 by PA-28R-201 Arrow 3. Production ceased in 1982 and restarted in 1990 with Arrow 3. Licence production by AICSA, Columbia, and CHINCUL, Argentinia, also by EMBRAER and NAIWA, Brazil, as EMB-711B/C Carisco.
