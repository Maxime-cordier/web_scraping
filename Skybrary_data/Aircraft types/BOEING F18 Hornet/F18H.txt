Multi role fighter. In service since 1979 (F/A-18C since 1986, F/A-18E since 1996). Combined development of F-18 fighter and A-18 ground attack aircraft. FA-18A/C single seat,. FA-18B/D tandem seat versions.. FA-18C/D improved development with new avionics and central computer (first flight in 1986).. New development (about 25% larger) FA-18E/F Super Hornet.. Used extensively by many European Air Forces and the US Navy. FA-18E/F: Wing span 13,62 m Length 18,31 m MTOW 29,950kg.
