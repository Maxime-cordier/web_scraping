The Soloy Pathfinder 21 is a twin engined, single propeller turboprop aircraft manufactured by the Soloy Corporation, and based on the successful Cessna 208 airframe. The aircraft is essentially a stock Cessna 208 airframe that has been stretched by 1.82 m, with structural reinforcement, and is powered by twin Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6D-114A engines in a DualPack (gearbox) configuration. Only one produced for certification purposes. Wing span (metric): 15.88 m. Length (metric): 14.30 m. Height (metric): 5.02 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6D-114A Soloy DualPack turboprop, 1329 hp (991.05 kW) combined output each.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6. 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada PT6D-114A Soloy DualPack turboprop, 1329 hp (991.05 kW) combined output each.
