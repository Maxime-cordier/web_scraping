The Learjet 24 is an American six to eight seat (two crew and four to six passengers) twin-engined, high speed business jet. Manufactured by Learjet as the successor to the Lear Jet 23. Wing span (metric): 10.84 m. Length (metric): 13.80 m. Height (metric): 3.73 m. Powerplant: 2 x General Electric CJ610-6 turbojet engines.. Engine Model(s): General Electric CJ610. 2 x General Electric CJ610-6 turbojet engines.
