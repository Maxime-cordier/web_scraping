Mid-size corporate jet. In service since 1993. Modified version of Learjet 55 with stretched fuselage and more powerful Pratt&Whitney engines. Largest member of Learjet family. The LJ60 is a member of the Bombardier Learjet Family. Wing span (metric): 13.40 m. Length (metric): 17.90 m. Height (metric): 4.50 m. Powerplant: 2 x 20.46 kN PWC PW 305 A turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PW300. 2 x 20.46 kN PWC PW 305 A turbofans.
