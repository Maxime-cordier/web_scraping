Ultra long-range business jet. First flight in November 2016. Received type certification in the second half of 2018. Entry into service in the end of 2018. The aircraft is marketed by the manufacturer as Global 7500 to reflect the range increase. Wing span (metric): 31.70 m. Length (metric): 33.90 m. Height (metric): 8.20 m. Powerplant: 2 x General Electric Passport turbofans, 16,500 lbf (73.4 kN) each. Engine Model(s): General Electric Passport. 2 x General Electric Passport turbofans, 16,500 lbf (73.4 kN) each
