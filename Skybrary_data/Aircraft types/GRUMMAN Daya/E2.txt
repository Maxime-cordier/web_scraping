Carrier and land-based AEW aircraft. In service since 1962 (E-2B since 1969, E-2C since 1970). Primary development E-2A for Vietnam war was upgraded to E-2B in 1969. The current version in service is the E-2C. Trainer version TE-2C. Used by US Navy and several services outside (France, Japan and others). Wing span (metric): 24.60 m. Length (metric): 17.60 m. Height (metric): 5.60 m. Powerplant: 2 x 5.100 SHP Allison T56-A-425 turboprops with 4 blade propellers.. 2 x 5.100 SHP Allison T56-A-425 turboprops with 4 blade propellers.
