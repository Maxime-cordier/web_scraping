Business aircraft. In service since 1970. Stretched replacement of the BEECH 55 Baron. Normally non-pressurized but BEECH 58P is pressurized. Turbocharged version BEECH 58TC. US-mil. type: T-42. This type has been taken over and is partly built by RAYTHEON, USA. Wing span (metric): 11.50 m. Length (metric): 9.10 m. Height (metric): 2.97 m. Powerplant: 2 x 300 HP Continental IO 550-C piston engines or 2 x 325 HP Continental TSIO 520-W3 turbocharged piston engines with 3 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Continental IO-550. 2 x 300 HP Continental IO 550-C piston engines or 2 x 325 HP Continental TSIO 520-W3 turbocharged piston engines with 3 blade propellers.
