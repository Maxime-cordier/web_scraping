The Mil Mi-26 (Russian Ми-26, NATO reporting name Halo) is a Soviet/Russian heavy transport helicopter in service in civilian and military roles. It is the largest and most powerful helicopter ever to have gone into production. Wing span (metric): 32.00 m. Length (metric): 40.03 m. Height (metric): 8.15 m. Powerplant: 2 x Lotarev D-136 turboshafts, 8,380 kW (11,240 shp) each.. Engine Model(s): Lotarev D-136. 2 x Lotarev D-136 turboshafts, 8,380 kW (11,240 shp) each.
