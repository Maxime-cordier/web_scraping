Light aircraft. In service since the early 80s. Member of SOCATA's Caribbean TB single engine aircraft family. More powerful, retractable gear version of TB-10 Tobago. TB-21 Trinidad TC is a turbocharged version. Note: The ICAO designator TRIN was deleted from ICAO Doc 8643 and replaced by TB20 and TB21 in 2014. Wing span (metric): 9.80 m. Length (metric): 7.70 m. Height (metric): 3.00 m. Powerplant: 1 x 250 HP Lycoming IO-540-C4D5D piston engine with 2 blade propeller. 1 x 250 HP Lycoming IO-540-C4D5D piston engine with 2 blade propeller
