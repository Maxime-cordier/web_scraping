The Piper PA-47 PiperJet is a single-engined very light jet, with a straight duct air intake design for the vertical stabilizer (tail) mounted engine. Wing span (metric): 13.49 m. Length (metric): 10.87 m. Height (metric): 4.78 m. Powerplant: 1 x Williams FJ44-3AP turbofan.. Engine Model(s): Williams FJ44. 1 x Williams FJ44-3AP turbofan.
