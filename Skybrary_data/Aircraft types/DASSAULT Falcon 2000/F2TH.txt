Long range, large size corporate jet. In service since 1995. Twin engine development of long range Falcon 900. Shares the 900s wings and fuselage with modern twin jet design. Wing span (metric): 19.30 m. Length (metric): 20.20 m. Height (metric): 7.10 m. Powerplant: 2 x GE CFE738-1-1B (26.7kN) turbofans.. Engine Model(s): General Electric CFE738. 2 x GE CFE738-1-1B (26.7kN) turbofans.
