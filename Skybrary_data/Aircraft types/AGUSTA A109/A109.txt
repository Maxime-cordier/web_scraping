Twin engined utility and corporate helicopter. About 80 were produced between 1976 and 1980. Wing span (metric): 11.00 m. Length (metric): 13.08 m. Height (metric): 3.32 m. Powerplant: 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada 206C or Turboméca Arrius 2K1 turboshafts, 423 kW or 426 kW each.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney Canada PW200. 2 x Pratt & Whitney Canada 206C or Turboméca Arrius 2K1 turboshafts, 423 kW or 426 kW each.
