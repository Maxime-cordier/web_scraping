The AH-1 Cobra (company designation: Bell 209) is a two-bladed, single engine attack helicopter manufactured by Bell. It shares a common engine, transmission and rotor system with the older UH-1 Iroquois. The AH-1 is also referred to as the HueyCobra or Snake. Wing span (metric): 13.60 m. Length (metric): 16.10 m. Height (metric): 4.10 m. Powerplant: 1 x Lycoming T53-L-703 turboshaft, 1,800 shp (1,300 kW). Engine Model(s): Lycoming T53. 1 x Lycoming T53-L-703 turboshaft, 1,800 shp (1,300 kW)
