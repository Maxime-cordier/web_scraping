Regional airliner. In service since 1961. Developed as the AVRO 748, transferred to HAWKER SIDDELEY as HS-748 in 1959. Built in several series: most advanced variant Super 748 (first flight July 1984) with advanced flightdeck and improved efficiency. Derivative version is BAe ATP. Military version is called Hawker Siddeley Andover (HS 780) but only a handful of these are still used. Super 748: Wingspan 31,23 m. Wing span (metric): 30.00 m. Length (metric): 20.40 m. Height (metric): 7.60 m. Powerplant: 2 x 2.280 SHP R-R Dart Mk 534 or 2 x 2.280 SHP R-R Dart Mk 552 turboprops with 4 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Rolls-Royce Dart. 2 x 2.280 SHP R-R Dart Mk 534 or 2 x 2.280 SHP R-R Dart Mk 552 turboprops with 4 blade propellers.
