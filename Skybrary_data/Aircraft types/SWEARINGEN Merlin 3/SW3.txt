The Swearingen Merlin or the Fairchild Aerospace Merlin is a pressurised, twin turboprop business aircraft first produced by Swearingen Aircraft, and later by Fairchild at a plant in San Antonio, Texas. In service since 1970. Development of SWEARINGEN Merlin 2 with a new tail. Improved version with winglets Fairchild 300. 92 Merlin 3 and 10 Merlin 300 were built. Wing span (metric): 14.10 m. Length (metric): 12.90 m. Height (metric): 5.10 m. Powerplant: 2 x 840 SHP Garrett TPE331-10U-503G turboprops with 4 blade propellers.. Engine Model(s): Garrett AiResearch TPE-331. 2 x 840 SHP Garrett TPE331-10U-503G turboprops with 4 blade propellers.
