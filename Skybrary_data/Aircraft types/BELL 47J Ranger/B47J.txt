The Bell 47J Ranger is a two-bladed, single engine, light helicopter manufactured by Bell Helicopter. It is based on the highly successful Bell 47. Wing span (metric): 11.32 m. Length (metric): 9.63 m. Height (metric): 2.83 m. Powerplant: 1 x Lycoming TVO-435-F1A flat, six-cylinder, reciprocating engine, 280 hp (210 kW).. Engine Model(s): Lycoming O-435. 1 x Lycoming TVO-435-F1A flat, six-cylinder, reciprocating engine, 280 hp (210 kW).
