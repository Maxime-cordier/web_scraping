Strategic transport aircraft. In service since 1965. USAs first purpose designed jet powered strategic freighter. Equipped for in-flight refuelling. Stretched version: C-141B. NC-141 with shorter fuselage for various test duties. Wing span (metric): 48.80 m. Length (metric): 51.30 m. Height (metric): 11.80 m. Powerplant: 4 x 93.4 kN GE JT3D (USAF: TF33-P-7) turbofans.. Engine Model(s): Pratt & Whitney JT3D. 4 x 93.4 kN GE JT3D (USAF: TF33-P-7) turbofans.
