The Bell 430 is a twin-engined light-medium helicopter built by Bell Helicopter Textron. It is a stretched and more powerful development of the Bell 230, which, in turn, was based on the earlier Bell 222. Wing span (metric): 12.80 m. Length (metric): 13.44 m. Height (metric): 3.73 m. Powerplant: 2 x Rolls-Royce 250-C40B 783 hp (584 kW).. Engine Model(s): Rolls-Royce 250. 2 x Rolls-Royce 250-C40B 783 hp (584 kW).
