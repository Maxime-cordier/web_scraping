The Eurocopter SA 365/AS365 Dauphin 2 (Dolphin) is a medium-weight multipurpose twin-engine helicopter manufactured by Eurocopter (originally by Aerospatiale). Wing span (metric): 11.50 m. Length (metric): 13.20 m. Height (metric): 3.50 m. Powerplant: 2 x Turboméca Arriel 2C turboshafts, 625 kW (838 shp) each.. Engine Model(s): Turboméca Arriel. 2 x Turboméca Arriel 2C turboshafts, 625 kW (838 shp) each.
