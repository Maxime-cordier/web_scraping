Monsoon-influenced hot-summer humid continental climate; coldest month averaging below 0 °C, at least one month's average temperature above 22 °C, and at least four months averaging above 10 °C. At least ten times as much rain in the wettest month of summer as in the driest month of winter (alternative definition is 70% or more of average annual precipitation is received in the warmest six months). Examples of airports with Dwa climate include Gimpo, Seoul, South Korea, Pyongyang, North Korea, and Dali, Yunnan, China.
