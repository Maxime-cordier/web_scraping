In the winter months, give consideration to de-icing operations on the ground and the impact of that on turn-around times. Give consideration to the impact of extreme cold on all aspects of the operation e.g. exposure of personnel to the cold during walkarounds, loading and unloading,. cold soaking of aircraft if on the ground for any length of time may cause numerous mechanical and electrical system issues such as battery function, freezing of hydraulic fluid and liquid water in galley and toilet systems, locked brakes.. Runways may be contaminated year round. Temperature error correction will almost certainly be required to heights on approach.
