“Risk management. The identification, analysis and elimination (and/or mitigation to an acceptable or tolerable level) of those hazards, as well as the subsequent risks, that threaten the viability of an organisation.” (ICAO Doc 9859). “Safety risk management (SRM) - A process within the Safety Management System composed of describing the system, identifying the hazards, and analyzing, assessing, and controlling the risk.” (FAA AC No: 120-92B, Safety Management Systems for Aviation Service Providers)
