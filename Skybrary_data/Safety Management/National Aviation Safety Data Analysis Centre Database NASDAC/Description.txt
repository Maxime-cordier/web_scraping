National Aviation Safety Data Analysis Centre Database (NASDAC) contains data on accidents, incidents, air traffic facility information, air traffic control regulations and procedures, aviation safety studies, etc. National Aviation Safety Data Analysis Centre Database (NASDAC) databases, being linked to related databases, often have gone through a cleansing process. The National Aviation Safety Data Analysis Centre Database (NASDAC) system has the capability to construct customised data marts. This allows aircraft engineers to monitor the safety performance of ageing aircraft. National Aviation Safety Data Analysis Centre Database (NASDAC) data can be accessed directly by the requestor through an easy-to-use data retrieval system. An expert staff is available to assist in determining the data that is needed and how to retrieve and analyse it. National Aviation Safety Data Analysis Centre Database (NASDAC) is located at the U.S. FAA headquarters, but it can be accessed either in person, by telephone, or on the web.
