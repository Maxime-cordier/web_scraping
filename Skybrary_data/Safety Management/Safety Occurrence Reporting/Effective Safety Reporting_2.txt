As a consequence of key safety data being properly safeguarded, and the promotion of a system of checks and balances that ensures that reporters of hazards feel confident that hazard reporting will not be put to uses others than for which it was implemented, operational personnel are encouraged (and rewarded) for providing essential safety information related to hazards.
