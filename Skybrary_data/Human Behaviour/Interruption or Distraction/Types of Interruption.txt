Interruption on the flight deck may result from causes within the aircraft, e.g. the occupant(s) of (the) flight deck supernumary crew seat (s), the activation of an caution or warning, the activation of a cabin crew call alert, unexpected information from ATC or the operating Company. Interruption may also occur in the control room, e.g due to a telephone call, a message from a colleague or detection of a potential airspace infringement or level bust.
