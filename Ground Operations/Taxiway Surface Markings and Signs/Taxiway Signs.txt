Signs are classified as either ‘Mandatory’ or ‘Information’ and are designed to provide simple surface movement guidance and control especially when Low Visibility Procedures(LVP) are in force. Both types are lit either internally or externally for night and low visibility operations where an airport operates under LVP, and may be lit for night operations at airports which do not operate under LVP. Mandatory Signs have white characters on a red background and require a specific ATC clearance to a point beyond them before they are passed. They are of three types: Information Signs have a combination of yellow and black markings and are provided at the discretion of the airport operator to provide additional guidance to taxiing aircraft.
