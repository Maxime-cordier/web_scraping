Loaded ULD must always be stored on a suitable transfer vehicle such as: a Dolly. a slave pallet. Container racking. within a Cargo terminal ULD handling and storage system. on a purpose-built road vehicle or railway wagon. and only be transferred using special purpose equipment equipped with rollers/ ball mats or similar support/ transfer devices. Specifications for most of these equipments may be found in the IATA Airport Handling Manual. Chapter 9. Poor handling may well result in a ULD, which was perfectly serviceable when loading commenced arriving at the aircraft door in an unserviceable state, resulting in off loads, delays , reloads and other inconveniences.
