As a controller, one can expect that pilots who become aware of a bomb threat will request: Other unexpected requests may also be made as a result of co-ordination with other agencies. A TWR/APP controller should expect the aircraft to be put into landing configuration earlier than usual and track miles should never be minimised unless this is expressly requested. Do not allow a missed approach to become necessary because of ATC reasons.
