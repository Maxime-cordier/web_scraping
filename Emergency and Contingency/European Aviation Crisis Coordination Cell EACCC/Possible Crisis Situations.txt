A non-exhaustive list of possible threats adversely affecting, directly or indirectly, aviation which can be declared as a crisis and would involve activation of the EACCC includes the following: volcanic ash dispersions, nuclear emissions dispersions, armed conflict, hazardous chemicals events, fire, security incidents (terrorism), airborne spread of diseases/pandemic, earthquakes, flooding, major failure of a pan-European function, industrial action or unavailability of several air navigation service providers, massive cyberattack, severe meteorological situations, shortage of fuel in Europe, and threats from space (e.g. satellite, meteorite, etc.).
