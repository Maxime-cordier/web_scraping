For further information on Contingency Planning see the EUROCONTROL, Guidelines for Contingency Planning for Air Navigation Services (including Service Continuity). In particular, Chapter 5 of edition 2 deals with the legal aspects of contingency including State Responsibility, Liability, Insurance and Sovereignty.. Legal and Regulatory Issues of Contingency Relation between Contingency and Law, Legal Framework, Actors concerned and their respective responsibilities and also Specific Aspects can be found in this presentation by J.Balsera Goni, EUROCONTROL.
