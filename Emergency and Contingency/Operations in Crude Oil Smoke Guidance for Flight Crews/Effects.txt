Severe Turbulence can be expected in a pyrocumulous cloud and the cloud may contain ash from the fire.. Operation of engines and aircraft systems should not be immediately adversely affected by the smoke, but prolonged exposure will lead to airframe and systems contamination.. Smoke or Fumes in the flight deck or cabin may result in breathing discomfort and /or irritation of the eyes.. Reduced forward visibility in the cloud and reduced visibility due to contamination of windscreens.. Reduced range of nav/comm radios. Possible contamination of pitot static system causing unreliable airspeed indications.
