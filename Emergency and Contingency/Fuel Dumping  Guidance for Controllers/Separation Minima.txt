ICAO Doc 4444 PANS-ATM specifies the following separation minima to be used in respect to other known traffic in case of fuel dumping: The horizontal boundaries of the area within which other traffic requires appropriate vertical separation extend for 10 NM either side of the track flown by the aircraft which is dumping fuel, from 10 NM ahead, to 50 NM or 15 minutes along track behind it (including turns).
