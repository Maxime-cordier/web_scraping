Best practice is to follow the following guidelines using the mnemonic ASSIST: A - Ensure that the reported emergency is well-understood and acknowledged; S - Establish and maintain separation from other traffic and terrain; S - Impose silence on your control frequency, if necessary; and do not delay or disturb urgent cockpit action by unnecessary transmissions; I - Inform your supervisor and other sectors, units and airports as appropriate; S - Provide maximum support to the flight crew; and, T - Allow the flight crew sufficient time to manage the emergency.
