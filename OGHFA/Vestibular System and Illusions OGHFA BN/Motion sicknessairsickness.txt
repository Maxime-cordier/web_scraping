Motion sickness in flight is termed airsickness. Air sickness is a normal response of some healthy individuals when exposed to a flight environment characterised by unfamiliar motion and orientation cues. Causes: Motion sickness arises from conflicting or mismatched sensory input (e.g., visual, vestibular and proprioceptive pathways).
