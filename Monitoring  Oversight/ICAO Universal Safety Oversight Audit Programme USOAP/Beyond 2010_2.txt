Anticipating the applicability in November 2019 of Amendment 1 to Annex 19, ICAO allowed a number of States — on a voluntary but non-confidential basis — to be the first to experience USOAP CMA audits during the rest of 2018 as well as 2019 on that basis. Currently, ICAO conducts regional workshops for Contracting States that emphasise the various aspects of the USOAP CMA. The objectives of the workshops are to provide States with updated information on the programme and provide hands-on training on the OLF.
