See Fire in the Air and guidance for flight crew and air traffic controllers: Cabin Crew need to be especially alert to the possibility of the Aircraft Fire Risk from Battery-powered Items Carried on Aircraft The special case of Wing Fire is usually the result of fuel fed fire originating directly or indirectly from the malfunction of engines embedded in the wing but may also arise when wing mounted engines suffer an uncontained failure and wing damage is caused by the ejected debris. Fire in the aircaft cabin is covered in Passenger Cabin Fire FAA Research Reports
