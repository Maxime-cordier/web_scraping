A type certificate is issued by the National Aviation Authority (NAA) of the State of the Operator stating the airworthiness standard for the aircraft type, model, aircraft engine or aircraft propeller. Some States use a different term for type certificate, such as type approval certificate, certificate of approval, or Fiche de Navigabilit√©.
