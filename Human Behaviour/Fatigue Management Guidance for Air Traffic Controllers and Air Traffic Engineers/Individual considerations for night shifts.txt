Night shifts challenge your normal ‘body clock’ (internal circadian rhythms). Naturally, you will feel most sleepy in the early hours of the morning (03.00-05.00). Individual needs for sleep and rest will differ with age and with general health. If you feel that this may affect your ability to drive home, you should consider sleeping before your journey home.
