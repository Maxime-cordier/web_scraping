Sleep is vital for your body to rest and repair as well as to give your brain a chance to ‘sort things out’ – it is thought sleep may be the time when the brain organises and stores information, replaces chemicals, and solves problems. A lack of good quality sleep will affect you. Most people require between 7 and 8 hours good quality sleep within each 24 hour period.
