An unexpected happening mid-way through a checklist can cause distraction and either failure to complete the checklist or the checklist actions not being completed correctly. In such a circumstance it is advisable to start the checklist from the beginning.. Indirect effects: The unexpected event may have occurred on the flight deck of an aircraft ahead or in the ATC control room. Start "tidying up". create spare mental capacity to be ready to deal with any ramifications that will affect your flight..
