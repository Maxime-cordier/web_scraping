Breakdown in teamwork may have an adverse effect on the following: Communication between controllers, including briefing on handover;. Communication between controllers and pilots;. Situational awareness;. Decision making;. Monitoring of pilot actions;. Monitoring of colleagues;. Cooperation with other controllers;. Distribution of workload between controllers;. Flexibility - ability to adjust to changing workload;. Any or all of these factors taken singly or in combination may contribute to an accident or serious incident. Additionally, breakdown in teamwork may lead to: Frustration and irritation;. Low morale and poor job-satisfaction;. which are likely to impact on team performance (the vicious circle).
