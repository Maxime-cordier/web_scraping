Situational awareness is so important that much of the working environment of both the pilot and the ATCO is designed and used to help maintain it. For the ATCO, this includes communication, with aircraft (voice and data link) and with other controllers; and radar, together with associated safety nets.. For the pilot, this includes communication with the controller; monitoring communication between controllers and other aircraft; visual look-out; navigation (incuding use of maps and charts); and aircraft safety nets, including TAWS, ACAS and GPWS..
