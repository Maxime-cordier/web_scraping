The Regulation entered into force in May 2021 and will be applicable from 26 January 2023. This Regulation was amended by Regulation 2023/203. The changes add provisions for information security risk assessment, information security management and reaction to information security vulnerabilities and incidents and are applicable as of 22.02.2026.
