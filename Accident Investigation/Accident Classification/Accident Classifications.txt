Hull Loss Accident: An accident in which the aircraft damage is not repairable, or is damaged but not repaired. Hull loss accidents include when the aircraft is missing, or if the wreckage is inaccessible. Major Accident: An accident in which any of the following three conditions are met: Fatal Accident: An accident causing one or more fatalities to occupants of the aircraft Substantial Damage Accident: An accident in which the aircraft sustained substantial damage Serious Accident: An accident in which either of the following two conditions are met: Minor Accident: An accident in which the aircraft sustained minor damage Aircraft incident: An occurrence other than an accident that affects or could affect the safety of operations
