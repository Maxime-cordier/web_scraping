In flight it can be difficult to distinguish between the symptoms of hyperventilation from those of hypoxia. Therefore, the worst case must be assumed and the response must be the same as for hypoxia: follow your approved procedures for hypoxia in line with your training. Such drills may cover the following points: Above 10,000ft altitude, if one crewmember is Hyperventilating, the other crewmembers should also assume it could be due to Hypoxia and also complete the Hypoxia response. It is usual to don your own mask and establish oxygen flow before helping others.
