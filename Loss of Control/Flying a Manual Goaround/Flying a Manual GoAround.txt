"For a safe go-around," the FSF briefing note continues, "the following “three Ps” constitute a golden rule: Pitch Set and maintain the pitch-attitude target;. Power Set and check the go-around thrust; and,. Performance Check aircraft performance: positive rate of climb, airspeed at or above Vref (reference landing speed), speed brakes retracted, radio-altimeter indications and barometric-altimeter indications increasing, wings level, gear up, flaps as required..
