While areas of strength are easy for the organisation to accept, areas of unexpected weakness can potentially cause an overreaction if not properly managed. The NOSS report is merely a diagnostic overview of what is occurring during normal operations and does not indicate solutions. For meaningful safety change to occur, the organisation must determine why particular patterns of threats, errors and undesired states are occurring.
