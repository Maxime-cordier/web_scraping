The U.S. Federal Aviation Administration’s (FAA’s) website, as of 2021, defines SMS, and cites the national policy order and regulatory requirements for FAA and U.S. Part 121 air carriers to implement an SMS. Governing the FAA’s own airline-oversight practices, “SMS is the formal, top-down, organization-wide approach to managing safety risk and assuring the effectiveness of safety risk controls. It includes systematic procedures, practices, and policies for the management of safety risk.” (FAA National Policy Order 8000.369C, Safety Management System, June 24, 2020).
