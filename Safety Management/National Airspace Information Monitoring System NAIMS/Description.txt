National Airspace Information Monitoring System (NAIMS) is a Federal Aviation Administration program to collect, maintain and analyse aviation statistical information based on reports of accidents and incidents in the US national airspace system. National Airspace Information Monitoring System (NAIMS) produces a monthly report available to the public, supplies data to National Aviation Safety Data Analysis Centre Database (NASDAC), and responds to public inquiries for safety information.
