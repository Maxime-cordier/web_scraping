While ground damage incidents cannot be eliminated, their number can be reduced and impact on the safety of subsequent operations can be mitigated by: Establishing and maintaining a reporting culture. If people are reluctant to report, problems may remain hidden for a long time ultimately resulting in or contributing to an accident.. Establishing of clear and unambiguous procedures, including provisions for the rectification of sub-optimal procedures.. Reduction of commercial pressure. The line between safety and efficiency should be clear as well as personnel's priorities. In general, the need of trade-off between safety and efficiency should not occur.. Hazard identificaiton during investigations (instead of mostly focusing on the human error)..
