B741, en-route, Pacific Ocean, 1997 - on 28th December 1997, a B747, operated by United Airlines, encountered severe turbulence thought to have been associated with a Jet Stream over the Pacific Ocean. Several passengers and crew members sustained serious injuries and one passenger was killed. For further information, see NTSB Final Report DCA98MA015. Bureau d’Enquêtes et d’Analyses (BEA) Incidents in Air Transport No 5 - Wind Gradients and Turbulence. Meteorological Office; Handbook of Aviation Meteorology, 3rd Edition, HMSO London, 1994..
