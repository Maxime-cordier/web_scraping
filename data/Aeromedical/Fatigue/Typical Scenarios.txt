In a pilot, fatigue may manifest itself by: Inaccurate flying;. Missed radio calls;. Symptoms of equipment malfunctions being missed;. Routine tasks being performed inaccurately or even forgotten; and, in extreme cases,. Falling asleep - either a short "micro-sleep" or for a longer period.. In an air traffic controller, fatigue may result in: Poor decision making;. Slow reaction to changing situation;. Failure to notice an impending confliction;. Loss of situational awareness;. Forgetfulness.. Fatigue is also a signficant risk among aviation maintenance personnel due to nighttime work, rotating shift work,and long, unregulated duty times, among other factors.
