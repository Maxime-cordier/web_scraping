HeliOffshore is a U.K.-based, safety-focused association representing the offshore helicopter industry. Its stated mission is "to lead a collective safety conversation, identifying the right priorities, supported by the right resources, delivering the right actions, to transform frontline safety performance." Resources available from HeliOffshore include a recommended practices and guide sfor health and usage monitoring systems, flightpath management, flight data monitoring, wind farm safety and human hazard analysis. HeliOffshore also publishes an annual safety performance report.
