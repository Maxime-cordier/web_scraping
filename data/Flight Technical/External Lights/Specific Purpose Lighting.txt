Logo Lights - Logo lights are generally mounted on the upper surface of the horizontal stabilizer and are aimed to illuminate the Company markings on the tail of the aircraft.. Search Lights - Enforcement aircraft, such as police helicopters, and search and rescue aircraft may be equiped with high intensity search lights.. Formation Lights - Some military aircraft have variable intensity lights installed on the upper surface of the wings to facilitate night formation flights, These lights may be in the visible or infrared spectrum..
