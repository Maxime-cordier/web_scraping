This SAFOPS WebEx, attended by 113 professionals from various aviation fields, was focused on the theme of "the wellbeing of aviation professionals". The WebEx was structured as follows: Session 1: An aviation professional's guide to wellbeing. Session 2: CLEAR TO CALM: Mindfulness in ATM. Session 3: Behaviour Change Psychology and Ergonomics for Human Wellbeing. Session 4: Energy and Resilience Management @MUAC.
