Normal checklists are of vital safety importance because they promote effective and efficient crew coordination and cross-checking. The judicious and timely initiation and completion of normal checklists is a highly effective means of preventing the omission of needed actions or the performance of inappropriate steps. Explicit standard calls should be defined in the SOPs for the interruption (hold) and resumption (continuation) of a normal checklist to address the situation of an intended pause or an interruption such as might be caused by a distraction.
