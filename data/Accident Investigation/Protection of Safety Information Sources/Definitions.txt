Safety information - information contained in safety data collection and processing systems (SDCPS) established for the sole purpose of improving aviation safety, and qualified for protection under specified conditions. (ICAO Annex 13) Inappropriate use - use of safety information for purposes different from the purposes for which it was collected, namely, use of the information for disciplinary, civil, administrative and criminal proceedings against operational personnel, and/or disclosure of the information to the public.
