Training and awareness. The rating training phase usually includes some exercises focused on operations during adverse weather. In addition, the ANSPs should consider introducing seasonal refresher training (e.g. before the start of the summer season). This should improve ATCO performance when encountering actual or potential loss of separation during weather avoidance scenarios.. Workload management.
