A Mid-Air Collision (MAC) is an accident where two aircraft come into contact with each other while both are in flight. Events where aircraft collide on the runway or while one is on the ground and the other in the air close to the ground are covered under Runway Incursion.. Events where aircraft collide during taxi or push-back (including collisions with parked aircraft) are covered under Ground Operations.. Events where aircraft collide with obstacles (e.g. terrain, buildings, masts, trees etc.) while in flight are covered under CFIT..
