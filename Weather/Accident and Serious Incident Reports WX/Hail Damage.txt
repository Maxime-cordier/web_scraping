A321, en-route, Gimpo South Korea, 2006On 9 June 2006, an Airbus 321-100, operated by Asiana Airlines, encountered a thunderstorm accompanied by Hail around 20 miles southeast of Anyang VOR at an altitude of 11,500 ft, while descending for an approach to Gimpo Airport. The radome was detached and the cockpit windshield was cracked due to impact with Hail. A321, en-route, Vienna Austria, 2003On 26th May 2003, a British Midland A321 suffered severe damage from hail en route near Vienna.
