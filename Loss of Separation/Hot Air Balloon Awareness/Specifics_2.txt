Communication - Normally, balloonists communicate with their retrieve teams and with other balloons using hand-held radios. The same radios are used for ATC communications, when required. Some commercial balloons that are regularly operated in and around controlled airspace carry a lightweight Mode-S transponder. However, SSR equipment is not yet common in balloons..
