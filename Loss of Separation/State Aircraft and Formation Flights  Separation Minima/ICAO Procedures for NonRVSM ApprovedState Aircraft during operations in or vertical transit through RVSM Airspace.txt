According to provisions in "Procedures for Air Navigation Services - Air Traffic Management" (PANS-ATM Doc 4444): 1. During operations in or vertical transit through RVSM airspace with aircraft not approved for RVSM operations, pilots shall report non-approved status using the phraseology NEGATIVE RVSM (with supplementary information if applicable, e.g. State Aircraft) as follows: a) at initial call on any channel within RVSM airspace; b) in all requests for level changes; and c) in all read-backs of level clearances. 2. Air traffic controllers shall explicitly acknowledge receipt of messages from aircraft reporting RVSM non-approved status.
