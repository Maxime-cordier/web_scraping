The mechanism of the blind spot occurrence by definition is “controller not detecting a conflict”, i.e. inadequate attention and situational awareness. Attention is a limited resource and there are numerous processes that compete for it. It consists of two elements – vigilance (maintaining awareness) and focus (concentration on the task) which may be affected by: Competition for the attention resources from other tasks (particularly during high workload scenarios), distractions, attempts to remember.. Erosions of the attention resources by filtering mechanisms and physiological factors like fatigue..
