Examples of such fields are cleared level, estimated level at the AoR boundary, heading, speed or vertical speed instructions, direct route clearance, etc. These may vary considerably from system to system, both as availability and as position in the label.. Important procedure-related information is often added, e.g. the aircraft RVSM status, wake turbulence category, RNAV capability, etc..
