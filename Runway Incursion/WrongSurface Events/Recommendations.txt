IATA, EASA and FAA all have made recommendations for pilots, airlines and air traffic controllers to mitigate the risk of wrong-/incorrect-surface events. Among the recommendations are: Maintaining situational awareness;. Flying a stabilised approach;. Adhering to standard operating procedures and applying crew resource management to continuously monitor visual and instrument references during approach;. Using automation levels appropriate to the situation;. Planning and briefing the approach;. Identifying airports where the risk of such events may be elevated; and,. Being prepared to go around..
