The definition of “small aerodrome” varies from country to country. While there is no strict rule under which any given aerodrome is categorised as being "small", the following features are typical: Runway length is 1000m or less;. The aerodrome serves mostly light aircraft (MTOW of 7000kg or less);. Number of movements is relatively small. Limited provision of service (ATC, MET, etc.). Most traffic flies under VFR.
